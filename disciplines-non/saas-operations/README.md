# SaaS Operations

The SaaS Operations discipline covers SaaS product operations, platform management, multi-tenant infrastructure, and operational excellence for SaaS applications.

## Overview

This discipline ensures SaaS products are operated efficiently, platform reliability is maintained, multi-tenant environments are properly managed, and operational best practices are followed.

## Key Responsibilities

- SaaS platform operations management
- Multi-tenant infrastructure oversight
- Service reliability and uptime
- Performance monitoring and optimization
- Incident response and management
- Operational automation
- Deployment pipeline management
- Compliance and security operations
