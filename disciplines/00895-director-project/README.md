# 00895 - Project Director

The Project Director discipline covers end-to-end project leadership, delivery excellence, and cross-functional coordination for complex construction and engineering initiatives.

## Overview

This discipline ensures project success through strategic leadership, effective resource management, risk mitigation, and stakeholder engagement while maintaining quality standards and delivery timelines.

## Key Responsibilities

- Strategic project leadership
- Cross-functional coordination
- Delivery excellence assurance
- Quality standards oversight
- Client relationship management
- Team leadership and development
- Change management
- Continuous improvement
