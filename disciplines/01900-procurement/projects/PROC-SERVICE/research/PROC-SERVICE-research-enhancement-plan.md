# PROC-SERVICE Research Enhancement Plan

## Overview

This document outlines the research and enhancement plan for the Service Order Management Workflow project.

## Research Areas

### 1. Service Specification Optimization
- **Objective**: Achieve >95% specification completeness
- **Research**: Best practices in service requirement definition
- **Enhancement**: Automated specification validation and templates

### 2. Provider Selection Intelligence
- **Objective**: Improve selection accuracy to >90%
- **Research**: Provider evaluation frameworks and methodologies
- **Enhancement**: AI-assisted provider matching and scoring

### 3. SLA Development Automation
- **Objective**: Streamline SLA creation and negotiation
- **Research**: SLA templates and negotiation patterns
- **Enhancement**: Template-based SLA generation

### 4. Delivery Monitoring Enhancement
- **Objective**: Achieve >95% SLA compliance monitoring
- **Research**: Real-time monitoring techniques
- **Enhancement**: Automated milestone tracking and alerts

### 5. Acceptance Workflow Optimization
- **Objective**: Improve acceptance timeliness to >90%
- **Research**: Acceptance testing best practices
- **Enhancement**: Streamlined acceptance workflows
