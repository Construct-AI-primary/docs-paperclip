# PROC-TRACK Research Enhancement Plan

## Purpose

This document outlines the research plan for enhancing the Order Tracking & Expediting Workflow with advanced features and optimizations.

## Research Areas

### 1. Order Tracking Enhancements

#### 1.1 Real-Time Tracking Technologies
- WebSocket integration for live updates
- Push notification technologies
- IoT integration possibilities

#### 1.2 Predictive Analytics
- Delay prediction models
- ETA optimization algorithms
- Risk scoring mechanisms

### 2. Delay Detection Research

#### 2.1 Pattern Recognition
- Machine learning for delay patterns
- Historical data analysis
- Supplier performance modeling

#### 2.2 Root Cause Analysis
- Automated RCA frameworks
- Impact assessment algorithms
- Mitigation strategy generation

### 3. Expediting Optimization

#### 2.1 Supplier Engagement
- Automated contact protocols
- Communication templates
- Response tracking

#### 2.2 Escalation Logic
- Threshold-based escalation
- Priority scoring
- Resource allocation

### 4. Communication Research

#### 4.1 Customer Preferences
- Multi-channel notification preferences
- Personalization strategies
- Frequency optimization

#### 4.2 Status Communication
- Clear status categorization
- ETA communication best practices
- Proactive vs reactive updates

## Research Timeline

| Phase | Research Area | Duration | Deliverables |
|-------|---------------|----------|--------------|
| 1 | Real-time tracking technologies | Week 1 | Technology assessment |
| 2 | Predictive analytics | Week 2 | ML model specifications |
| 3 | Supplier engagement | Week 3 | Engagement protocols |
| 4 | Customer communication | Week 4 | Communication framework |

## Implementation Recommendations

Based on research findings, the following enhancements are recommended:

1. **Immediate (Phase 1-2)**
   - WebSocket integration for real-time updates
   - Basic delay pattern matching
   - Email notification system

2. **Short-term (Phase 3-4)**
   - Predictive ETA models
   - Automated supplier contact
   - Multi-channel notifications

3. **Long-term (Phase 5+)**
   - ML-based delay prediction
   - Advanced analytics dashboard
   - AI-powered expediting recommendations
