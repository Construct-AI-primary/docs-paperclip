# PROC-SUPP Research Enhancement Plan

## Overview

This document outlines the research and enhancement plan for the Supplier Qualification & Performance Monitoring Workflow project.

## Research Areas

### 1. Supplier Registration Optimization
- **Objective**: Achieve >95% registration completeness
- **Research**: Best practices in supplier onboarding
- **Enhancement**: Automated registration validation and templates

### 2. Pre-Qualification Intelligence
- **Objective**: Improve qualification accuracy to >90%
- **Research**: Supplier evaluation frameworks and methodologies
- **Enhancement**: AI-assisted supplier matching and scoring

### 3. Compliance Automation
- **Objective**: Streamline compliance verification
- **Research**: Compliance verification patterns and automation
- **Enhancement**: Automated compliance checking and certification

### 4. Performance Monitoring Enhancement
- **Objective**: Achieve >98% monitoring accuracy
- **Research**: Real-time monitoring techniques
- **Enhancement**: Automated KPI tracking and alerts

### 5. Quality Incident Optimization
- **Objective**: Improve resolution time to <48 hours
- **Research**: Incident management best practices
- **Enhancement**: Streamlined incident workflows
