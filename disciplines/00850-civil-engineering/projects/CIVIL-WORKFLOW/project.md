# Civil Engineering Workflow Templates

This project contains standardized workflow templates for civil engineering disciplines, providing consistent processes for infrastructure development and construction projects.

## Project Scope

- Stormwater management systems
- Road and highway design
- Bridge structural engineering
- Utilities infrastructure
- Earthworks and excavation
- Surface mining operations
- Pipeline design and installation
- Water reticulation systems
- Tunnel engineering

## Key Deliverables

- 9 workflow templates covering major civil engineering processes
- Standardized documentation and compliance requirements
- Integration with procurement and project management systems
- Quality assurance and safety protocols

## Target Users

- Civil engineers
- Project managers
- Construction supervisors
- Regulatory compliance officers
- Procurement specialists
