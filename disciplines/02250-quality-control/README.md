# 02250 - Quality Control

The Quality Control discipline covers quality control testing, material verification, product inspection, and defect management for construction and engineering works.

## Overview

This discipline ensures materials and works meet specified quality requirements, testing is conducted properly, defects are identified and addressed, and quality records are maintained accurately.

## Key Responsibilities

- Material testing and verification
- Quality control testing
- Defect identification and management
- Hold point witness management
- Quality record keeping
- Laboratory coordination
- Specification compliance
- Root cause analysis
