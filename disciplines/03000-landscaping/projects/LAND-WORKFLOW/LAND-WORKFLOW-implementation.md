# LAND-WORKFLOW — Implementation Plan

## Executive Summary

This implementation plan defines the phased approach for developing Landscaping workflow templates and procedures for Paperclip. The plan covers 4 major workflows across 3 phases over an estimated 14-week implementation period.

## Implementation Overview

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Workflows | 4 |
| Phases | 3 |
| Estimated Duration | 14 weeks |
| Total Estimated Hours | 172-232 hours |
| Primary Company | GreenScape AI |
| Supporting Companies | EarthWorks AI, StructureForge AI |

---

## Phase 1: Design (Weeks 1-6)

**Objective**: Complete all landscape and irrigation design workflows and documentation.

### Phase 1 Issues

| Issue ID | Title | Priority | Estimated Hours | Assignee |
|----------|-------|----------|-----------------|----------|
| LAND-001 | Landscape Design | Critical | 60-80 | landscape-designer |
| LAND-002 | Irrigation Design | High | 24-32 | irrigation-specialist |

### Phase 1 Deliverables

#### LAND-001 Deliverables
1. Site analysis report template
2. Concept design presentation templates
3. Planting design layout templates
4. Landscape specification document
5. Plant schedule template with technical data
6. Material specification sheets
7. Mood board and visualization standards
8. Design review checklist

#### LAND-002 Deliverables
1. Irrigation zone layout template
2. Pipe network calculation sheets
3. Irrigation specification document
4. Controller specification template
5. Drainage design calculations
6. Water feature system design
7. Material and equipment schedules

### Phase 1 Dependencies
- Input from architectural site plan
- Topographic survey data
- Soil analysis results
- Project programme

## Risk Register

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Plant availability delays | Medium | High | Early nursery engagement, substitute species list |
| Weather impacts on softworks | High | Medium | Flexible programming, weather contingency |
| Structural wall design changes | Low | High | Early structural coordination, staged approvals |
| Water feature pump specs change | Medium | Medium | Hydraulic engineer involvement in design |
| Council landscape approval delays | Medium | High | Early engagement, pre-lodgement meetings |
| Material price escalation | Medium | Medium | Materials specified with alternatives, early ordering |
| Skilled labour availability | Medium | High | Early contractor engagement, training programmes |
| Coordination conflicts with other trades | Medium | Medium | Coordinated programme, regular site meetings |
