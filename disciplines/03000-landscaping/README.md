# 03000 - Landscaping

The Landscaping discipline covers landscape architecture, environmental enhancement, revegetation, and visual amenity management for construction and engineering projects.

## Overview

This discipline ensures landscapes are designed and implemented appropriately, environmental impacts are minimized through revegetation, visual amenity is maintained, and landscaping specifications are followed.

## Key Responsibilities

- Landscape design coordination
- Revegetation planning and implementation
- Environmental enhancement
- Visual amenity management
- Landscape maintenance planning
- Tree and vegetation protection
- Sustainability in landscaping
- Compliance with environmental approvals
