# 01750 - Legal

The Legal discipline covers legal affairs, contract review, dispute resolution, regulatory compliance, and intellectual property management for construction and engineering organizations.

## Overview

This discipline ensures legal compliance, protects organizational interests, manages legal risks, and provides legal support across all operations and projects.

## Key Responsibilities

- Contract review and negotiation
- Legal risk management
- Dispute resolution and litigation
- Regulatory compliance
- Intellectual property protection
- Corporate governance support
- Legal documentation
- Regulatory liaison
