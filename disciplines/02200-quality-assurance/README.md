# 02200 - Quality Assurance

The Quality Assurance discipline covers quality management systems, process improvement, audit management, and compliance assurance for construction and engineering projects.

## Overview

This discipline ensures quality management systems are effective, processes are continuously improved, audits are conducted properly, and quality standards are maintained across all operations.

## Key Responsibilities

- QMS development and maintenance
- Process quality assurance
- Internal and external audit management
- Quality procedure development
- Corrective and preventive actions
- Quality metrics and reporting
- Certification management
- Continuous improvement initiatives
