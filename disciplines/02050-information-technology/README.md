# 02050 - Information Technology

The Information Technology discipline covers IT systems, software development, infrastructure management, cybersecurity, and digital transformation for construction and engineering organizations.

## Overview

This discipline ensures IT systems are properly managed, cybersecurity is maintained, technology solutions support business needs, and digital transformation initiatives are implemented effectively.

## Key Responsibilities

- IT infrastructure management
- Software development and deployment
- Cybersecurity and data protection
- System integration and APIs
- Digital transformation initiatives
- IT service management
- Technology procurement
- Business system optimization
