# 01400 - Health

The Health discipline covers occupational health, employee wellness, medical services, and health surveillance for construction and engineering organizations.

## Overview

This discipline ensures employee health and wellbeing, maintains occupational health standards, implements health surveillance programs, and ensures regulatory compliance with health requirements.

## Key Responsibilities

- Occupational health management
- Health surveillance programs
- Employee wellness initiatives
- Medical fitness assessments
- Workplace health monitoring
- First aid and emergency medical response
- Health promotion programs
- Compliance with health regulations
