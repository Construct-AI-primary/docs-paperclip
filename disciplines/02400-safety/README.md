# 02400 - Safety

The Safety discipline covers occupational health and safety, hazard management, incident investigation, and safety compliance for construction and engineering projects.

## Overview

This discipline ensures safe working conditions, hazards are identified and controlled, incidents are investigated properly, and safety regulations are complied with throughout all project activities.

## Key Responsibilities

- Safety planning and risk assessment
- Hazard identification and control
- Incident investigation and reporting
- Safety training and induction
- PPE management
- Emergency response planning
- Safety compliance monitoring
- Contractor safety management
