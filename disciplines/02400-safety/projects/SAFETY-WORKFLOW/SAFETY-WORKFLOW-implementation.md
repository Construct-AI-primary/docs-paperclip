# SAFETY-WORKFLOW — Implementation Plan

## Executive Summary

This implementation plan defines the phased approach for developing Safety Management workflow templates and procedures for Paperclip. The plan covers 5 major workflows across 3 phases over an estimated 10-week implementation period.

## Implementation Overview

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Workflows | 5 |
| Phases | 3 |
| Estimated Duration | 10 weeks |
| Total Estimated Hours | 66-86 hours |
| Primary Company | SafetyForge AI |
| Supporting Companies | HealthForge AI, DevForge AI |

---

## Phase 1: Planning & Foundation (Weeks 1-3)

**Objective**: Establish core safety management infrastructure and hazard identification processes.

### Phase 1 Issues

| Issue ID | Title | Priority | Estimated Hours | Assignee |
|----------|-------|----------|-----------------|----------|
| SAFETY-001 | Hazard Identification & Risk Assessment | Critical | 16-20 | safety-manager |
| SAFETY-002 | Permit to Work | Critical | 12-16 | safety-manager |

### Phase 1 Deliverables

1. **SAFETY-001 Deliverables**
   - Hazard identification procedure document
   - Risk assessment matrix template (5x5)
   - Hazard register template
   - Control measure implementation plan
   - Risk monitoring schedule template
   - JSA/JHA template

2. **SAFETY-002 Deliverables**
   - Permit to work procedure
   - Hot work permit template
   - Confined space entry permit template
   - Electrical work permit template
   - Excavation permit template
   - Permit register and tracking log

### Phase 1 Dependencies
- None (foundation phase)

---

## Phase 2: Implementation & Response (Weeks 4-7)

**Objective**: Implement incident investigation and emergency response workflows.

### Phase 2 Issues

| Issue ID | Title | Priority | Estimated Hours | Assignee |
|----------|-------|----------|-----------------|----------|
| SAFETY-003 | Incident Investigation & Reporting | High | 14-18 | safety-manager |
| SAFETY-004 | Emergency Response | Critical | 14-18 | safety-manager |

### Phase 2 Deliverables

1. **SAFETY-003 Deliverables**
   - Incident reporting procedure
   - Incident report form template
   - Root cause analysis methodology guide
   - Corrective action plan template
   - Investigation report template
   - Lessons learned documentation process

2. **SAFETY-004 Deliverables**
   - Emergency response plan template
   - Emergency evacuation procedures
   - Emergency contact directory template
   - Emergency drill schedule template
   - Emergency equipment inspection checklists
   - First aid/CPR certification tracking

### Phase 2 Dependencies
- BLOCKED BY: SAFETY-001 (Hazard Identification)
- BLOCKED BY: SAFETY-002 (Permit to Work)

## Risk Register

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Regulatory changes requiring workflow updates | Medium | Medium | Build flexibility into workflows; quarterly review cycle |
| Resource availability constraints | Low | High | Early resource confirmation; cross-training |
| Integration with existing systems | Medium | Medium | Phased integration; pilot testing |
| Stakeholder resistance to new procedures | Medium | High | Early engagement; training sessions; pilot programs |
| Data accuracy in incident reporting | Medium | High | Clear definitions; training; validation checks |
