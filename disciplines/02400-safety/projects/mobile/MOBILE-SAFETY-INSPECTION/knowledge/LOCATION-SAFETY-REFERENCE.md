# Location-Specific Safety Inspection Reference

## Overview

This document provides location-specific safety inspection protocols for the MOBILE-SAFETY-INSPECTION mobile app. It adjusts standard inspection checklists based on environmental conditions encountered in construction projects.

## Environment-Specific Safety Protocols

### Desert/Arid Environments

**Key Safety Concerns:**
- Heat-related illness (heat stroke, heat exhaustion, heat cramps)
- Dehydration and electrolyte imbalance
- UV radiation exposure
- Sand/dust inhalation
- Wildfire risk
- Equipment overheating

**Mobile Inspection Checklist:**
```
□ Heat stress monitoring station operational
□ Drinking water stations replenished
□ Shade structures available and adequate
□ Work-rest cycles posted and followed
□ Wet bulb globe temperature (WBGT) monitored
□ Air cooling systems functioning
□ UV protection equipment available
□ Dust suppression active on site
□ Fire watch posted during hot work
□ Equipment cooling systems checked
```

**Mobile App Features:**
- WBGT reading input and alerts
- Work-rest timer notifications
- Dehydration symptom checklist
- Emergency cooling location maps

### Polar/Arctic Environments

**Key Safety Concerns:**
- Hypothermia and frostbite
- Ice and snow accumulation
- Reduced daylight hours
- Equipment cold-start failures
- Permafrost ground instability
- Whiteout conditions

**Mobile Inspection Checklist:**
```
□ Personnel heating units operational
□ Insulated PPE issued and worn
□ Walking surfaces de-iced and salted
□ Snow load monitoring active
□ Emergency warming stations available
□ Lighting adequate for limited daylight
□ Vehicle battery warming systems checked
□ Hypothermia treatment equipment accessible
□ Communication equipment tested
□ Emergency evacuation routes clear
```

**Mobile App Features:**
- Temperature alert thresholds
- Ice accumulation photo documentation
- Daylight remaining calculator
- Emergency shelter location maps

### Offshore/Marine Environments

**Key Safety Concerns:**
- Falls from height
- Drowning risk
- Marine wildlife encounters
- Vessel movement hazards
- Corrosion-related failures
- Wave and tide effects

**Mobile Inspection Checklist:**
```
□ Life jacket use enforced
□ Gangway securing verified
□ Fall protection anchors inspected
□ Marine weather forecast checked
□ Vessel movement exclusion zone established
□ Diving operation safety protocols followed
□ Corrosion damage documented
□ Wave/tide level monitoring active
□ Emergency life-saving equipment accessible
□ Radio communication tested
```

**Mobile App Features:**
- Marine weather integration
- GPS boundary for exclusion zones
- Tide and wave data display
- Emergency muster point locations

### High-Altitude/Mountainous Environments

**Key Safety Concerns:**
- Altitude sickness (AMS, HAPE, HACE)
- Reduced oxygen availability
- Steep terrain and falls
- Seismic activity
- Rockfall hazards
- Limited evacuation options

**Mobile Inspection Checklist:**
```
□ Altitude acclimatization schedule followed
□ Oxygen supplementation available
□ AMS symptom monitoring active
□ Medical evacuation plan confirmed
□ Rockfall protection measures in place
□ Seismic monitoring active
□ Steep terrain access controlled
□ Cable crane safety verified
□ Medevac helicopter landing zone clear
□ Communication equipment functional at altitude
```

**Mobile App Features:**
- Altitude tracking and alerts
- AMS symptom assessment guide
- Evacuation time estimation
- Mountain weather forecasts

### Heavy Rainfall/Tropical Environments

**Key Safety Concerns:**
- Lightning strikes
- Flooding and water hazards
- Mosquito-borne diseases
- High humidity effects
- Soil instability
- Slippery conditions

**Mobile Inspection Checklist:**
```
□ Lightning detection system active
□ 30-30 rule protocol understood
□ Flooding evacuation routes posted
□ Drainage systems cleared and functional
□ Mosquito repellent available
□ Disease prevention measures followed
□ Slip-resistant footwear enforced
□ Electrical equipment protected from water
□ Soil stability monitoring active
□ Emergency shelter locations known
```

**Mobile App Features:**
- Lightning proximity alerts
- Real-time rainfall tracking
- Flood zone mapping
- Disease risk indicators

### Urban/Constrained Environments

**Key Safety Concerns:**
- Public interface hazards
- Noise and vibration
- Utility conflicts
- Limited access
- Working hour restrictions
- Adjacent structure protection

**Mobile Inspection Checklist:**
```
□ Public exclusion barriers in place
□ Noise level monitoring active
□ Vibration monitoring operational
□ Utility locations confirmed
□ Adjacent structures surveyed
□ Working hours displayed and followed
□ Traffic management plan active
□ Dust and debris control effective
□ Emergency vehicle access maintained
□ Neighboring occupants notified
```

**Mobile App Features:**
- Noise level logging
- Vibration monitoring data
- Utility conflict photo documentation
- Working hour schedule tracking

## Emergency Response Integration

| Environment | Primary Emergency | Response Protocol |
|-------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Desert | Heat stroke | Cooling, hydration, medical evac |
| Arctic | Hypothermia | Warming, shelter, medical evac |
| Marine | Man overboard | Life saving, vessel rescue |
| High Altitude | AMS/HAPE | Descent, oxygen, medical evac |
| Tropical | Lightning strike | Seek shelter, CPR readiness |
| Urban | Structure failure | Evacuation, emergency services |
