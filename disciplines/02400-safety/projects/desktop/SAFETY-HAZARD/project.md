# SAFETY-HAZARD Project Charter

## Project Overview

**Project Name**: Hazard Identification and Risk Assessment Workflow Implementation
**Project Code**: SAFETY-HAZARD
**Discipline**: 02400 - Safety
**Business Criticality**: High - Fundamental safety requirement

## Project Scope

### In Scope
- Workplace hazard identification and documentation
- Risk assessment and prioritization methodology
- Control measure development and implementation
- Risk monitoring and review processes
- Safety training and awareness program integration

### Out of Scope
- Incident investigation and reporting (covered in SAFETY-INCIDENT)
- Personal protective equipment management (covered in SAFETY-PPE)
- Safety training program administration (covered in SAFETY-TRAINING)
- Emergency response planning (covered in SAFETY-EMERGENCY)

## Project Objectives

### Primary Objectives
1. **Hazard Identification**: Comprehensive workplace hazard identification system
2. **Risk Assessment**: Standardized risk assessment and prioritization
3. **Control Implementation**: Systematic control measure development and tracking
4. **Continuous Monitoring**: Ongoing risk monitoring and review processes

### Success Criteria
- [ ] 100% of high-risk areas assessed within 30 days of implementation
- [ ] Risk assessment completion time < 2 hours per assessment
- [ ] Control measure implementation tracking > 95% accuracy
- [ ] Risk register updated and maintained > 98% compliance
