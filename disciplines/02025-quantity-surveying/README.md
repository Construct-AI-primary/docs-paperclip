# 02025 - Quantity Surveying

The Quantity Surveying discipline covers measurement, cost planning, valuation, and financial management of construction works.

## Overview

This discipline ensures accurate measurement of works, provides cost advice, manages valuations and payments, and maintains financial control throughout project lifecycles.

## Key Responsibilities

- Quantities take-off and measurement
- Cost planning and estimating
- Bills of quantities preparation
- Interim valuations and payment certificates
- Final account preparation
- Cost reporting and forecasting
- Variation valuation
- Contract financial administration
