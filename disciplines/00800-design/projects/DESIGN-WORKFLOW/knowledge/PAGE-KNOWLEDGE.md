# DESIGN-WORKFLOW Page Knowledge

## Cross-Discipline Dependencies

The Design Management workflow is the central coordination hub for all engineering disciplines.

### Upstream Dependencies
- Client requirements and project brief
- Regulatory and code requirements
- EIA commitments (01000)

### Downstream Impacts
- All discipline workflows depend on design coordination outputs
- Construction scheduling (00300) depends on design deliverable dates
- Procurement (01900) depends on design specifications

## Key Terms

- **IDR**: Interdisciplinary Design Review — structured cross-discipline review
- **DEP**: Design Execution Plan — master plan for design deliverables
- **BEP**: BIM Execution Plan — defines BIM requirements and coordination
- **CCR**: Client Comment Response — formal response to client design comments
- **IFC**: Issued for Construction — approved drawings for construction use
- **IFR**: Issued for Review — drawings submitted for review/comment
- **IFA**: Issued for Approval — drawings submitted for formal approval
