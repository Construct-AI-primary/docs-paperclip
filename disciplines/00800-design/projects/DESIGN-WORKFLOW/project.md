# DESIGN-WORKFLOW — Design Management Workflow Project

## Project Scope

This project delivers standardized workflow templates for the Design Management discipline, covering the full design lifecycle from concept through construction support and as-built handover.

### In Scope
- Design coordination and interface management across all disciplines
- Interdisciplinary design review (IDR) process
- Design programme scheduling and deliverables tracking
- Design change control and revision management
- BIM coordination and clash detection workflows
- Design quality assurance and audit procedures
- Construction support and RFI response management
- As-built documentation and project handover

### Out of Scope
- Discipline-specific design calculations (handled by individual discipline workflows)
- Procurement workflows (handled by 01900-procurement)
- Construction execution workflows (handled by 00300-construction)

## Key Standards

- ISO 19650 — BIM information management
- PAS 1192 — BIM framework (UK)
- AACE Recommended Practices — Design deliverable management
- PMBOK Guide — Project management processes
- Client-specific design review and approval procedures
