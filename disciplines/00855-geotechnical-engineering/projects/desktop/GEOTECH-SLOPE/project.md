## Project Overview

**Project Code**: GEOTECH-SLOPE

**Business Criticality**: High - Safety and ground stability

## Project Scope

### In Scope
- Slope stability analysis and risk assessment
- Earthworks design and cut/fill optimization
- Retaining wall and shoring system design
- Erosion control and drainage design
- Ground improvement recommendations

### Out of Scope
- Construction execution (handled by construction management)
- Structural design of retaining walls (handled by structural engineering)

1. **Stability Analysis**: Automate slope stability calculations and analysis
2. **Risk Assessment**: Provide comprehensive stability risk evaluations
3. **Design Optimization**: Optimize earthworks and retaining systems
4. **Safety Assurance**: Ensure all designs meet safety standards
