# GEOTECH-FOUNDATION Project Charter

## Project Overview

**Project Name**: Foundation Design and Analysis Workflow Implementation
**Project Code**: GEOTECH-FOUNDATION
**Discipline**: 00855 - Geotechnical Engineering
**Business Criticality**: High - Structural safety and foundation integrity

## Project Scope

### In Scope
- Foundation type selection and preliminary design
- Bearing capacity analysis and verification
- Settlement and deformation calculations
- Foundation system optimization and cost analysis
- Geotechnical design report preparation

### Out of Scope
- Structural design of foundation elements (handled by structural engineering)
- Construction specifications (handled by construction management)
- Site investigation (covered in GEOTECH-SITE project)

## Project Objectives

### Primary Objectives
1. **Design Automation**: Automate foundation design calculations and analysis
2. **Safety Assurance**: Ensure all designs meet safety and regulatory standards
3. **Optimization**: Provide cost-effective foundation system recommendations
4. **Integration**: Seamless integration with structural design workflows

### Success Criteria
- [ ] Foundation designs meet all safety standards
- [ ] Settlement predictions accurate within 20% of actual
- [ ] Design process reduced by 30%
- [ ] Integration with structural engineering workflows complete
