# 00889 - Finance Director

The Finance Director discipline covers financial planning, budgeting, cost management, financial reporting, and fiscal oversight for construction and engineering projects.

## Overview

This discipline ensures fiscal responsibility, maintains financial compliance, optimizes capital allocation, implements cost control measures, and provides accurate financial forecasting while adhering to organizational policies and regulatory requirements.

## Key Responsibilities

- Financial planning and forecasting
- Budget development and management
- Cost control and analysis
- Financial reporting and compliance
- Capital expenditure management
- Cash flow optimization
- Risk-based financial monitoring
- Stakeholder financial communication
