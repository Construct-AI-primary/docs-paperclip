# 01850 - Other Parties

The Other Parties discipline covers stakeholder management, third-party relationships, joint venture coordination, and partnership governance for construction and engineering projects.

## Overview

This discipline ensures effective management of external parties, maintains productive relationships with partners, manages joint ventures, and coordinates with stakeholders across all project activities.

## Key Responsibilities

- Stakeholder identification and mapping
- Third-party relationship management
- Joint venture coordination
- Partnership governance
- Communication management
- Stakeholder engagement planning
- Partner performance monitoring
- Dispute resolution with parties
