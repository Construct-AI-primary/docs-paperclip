# 00888 - Procurement Director

The Procurement Director discipline covers strategic sourcing, vendor management, contract negotiation, and procurement operations oversight for construction and engineering projects.

## Overview

This discipline ensures compliance with procurement regulations, maintains vendor relationships, optimizes supply chain efficiency, and implements cost-saving initiatives while adhering to organizational policies and legal requirements.

## Key Responsibilities

- Strategic sourcing and vendor selection
- Procurement policy development and enforcement
- Contract negotiation and management
- Supply chain optimization
- Vendor performance monitoring
- Cost analysis and budget management
- Compliance with procurement regulations
- Stakeholder coordination
