# 01200 - Finance

The Finance discipline covers financial operations, accounting, payroll, invoicing, and fiscal management for construction and engineering projects.

## Overview

This discipline ensures accurate financial record-keeping, timely payments, proper invoicing, and financial compliance with organizational and regulatory standards.

## Key Responsibilities

- Financial accounting and bookkeeping
- Accounts payable and receivable
- Invoice processing and management
- Payroll processing
- Expense tracking and reimbursement
- Tax compliance and reporting
- Financial record maintenance
- Audit support
