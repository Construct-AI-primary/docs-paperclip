# 01100 - Ethics

The Ethics discipline covers business ethics, corporate governance, compliance, integrity management, and ethical decision-making frameworks for construction and engineering organizations.

## Overview

This discipline ensures ethical conduct across all operations, maintains integrity standards, implements anti-corruption measures, and fosters a culture of transparency and accountability.

## Key Responsibilities

- Business ethics policy development
- Corporate governance oversight
- Anti-corruption and bribery prevention
- Conflict of interest management
- Whistleblower protection
- Ethical decision-making frameworks
- Compliance monitoring
- Ethics training and awareness
