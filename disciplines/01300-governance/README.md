# 01300 - Governance

The Governance discipline covers organizational governance, policy management, regulatory compliance, and corporate accountability frameworks.

## Overview

This discipline ensures proper governance structures are in place, policies are followed, regulatory requirements are met, and organizational accountability is maintained.

## Key Responsibilities

- Governance framework development
- Policy development and management
- Regulatory compliance monitoring
- Board support and coordination
- Risk management oversight
- Compliance reporting
- Stakeholder accountability
- Governance training
