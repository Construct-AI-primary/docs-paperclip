# Shared Business Logic

## Overview

This directory contains shared business logic documentation for the Logistics workflow.

## Business Rules

### Inventory Management
1. All items must have unique SKU codes
2. Reorder triggers at 20% stock level
3. Quality checks required for incoming shipments

### Delivery Scheduling
1. Confirm address before scheduling
2. Allow 2-hour delivery windows
3. Track delivery status in real-time
