# 01600 - Local Content

The Local Content discipline covers local content requirements, local sourcing, indigenous participation, and community benefit programs for construction and engineering projects.

## Overview

This discipline ensures compliance with local content regulations, promotes local economic development, supports indigenous businesses, and implements community benefit initiatives.

## Key Responsibilities

- Local content compliance management
- Local sourcing facilitation
- Indigenous participation programs
- Community benefit initiatives
- Local supplier development
- Local content reporting
- Stakeholder engagement with communities
- Socio-economic impact monitoring
