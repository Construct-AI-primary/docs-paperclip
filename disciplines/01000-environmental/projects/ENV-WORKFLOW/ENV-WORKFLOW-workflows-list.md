## Section 1: Environmental Workflows Catalog

### 1. Environmental Impact Assessment Workflow (ENV-001)

**Status:** ⚠️ Partially Implemented  
**Platform:** Desktop  
**Source:** Environmental management knowledge sections on impact assessment and regulatory compliance

**Description:** Comprehensive workflow for conducting and managing Environmental Impact Assessments (EIAs) for development projects, ensuring compliance with local, national, and international environmental regulations while identifying and mitigating potential environmental impacts.

**Key Activities:**
- Scoping and screening of development projects for EIA requirements
- Baseline environmental condition assessment and data collection
- Impact identification, prediction, and evaluation methodologies
- Mitigation measure development and alternative analysis
- EIA report preparation and regulatory submission
- Public consultation and stakeholder engagement management
- Decision support and consent condition monitoring

**Deliverables:**
- Screening and scoping reports
- Baseline environmental surveys and assessments
- EIA technical appendices and specialist studies
- Environmental Impact Statement (EIS)
- Mitigation and monitoring plans
- Regulatory submissions and consent applications

**Standards & Guidelines:**
- ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management Systems
- ISO 14044 Life Cycle Assessment
- World Bank Environmental and Social Framework
- IFC Performance Standards on Environmental and Social Sustainability
- Local environmental protection laws and regulations

**Estimated Hours:** 800-1200 hours per project (varies by complexity)

**Business Criticality:** High - Regulatory compliance and environmental protection

### 2. Environmental Management Plan Workflow (ENV-002)

**Status:** ⚠️ Partially Implemented  
**Platform:** Desktop  
**Source:** Environmental management knowledge on EMP development and implementation

**Description:** Workflow for developing, implementing, and monitoring Environmental Management Plans (EMPs) that translate EIA findings into actionable environmental management strategies for construction and operational phases of projects.

**Key Activities:**

- Environmental aspect and impact identification
- Management measure specification and procedures
- Emergency response planning and spill containment
- Training requirements and awareness programs
- Inspection and monitoring schedule development
- EMP implementation tracking and reporting

**Deliverables:**
- Project-specific EMP document
- Environmental procedures and work instructions
- Emergency response plans and contacts
- Inspection checklists and audit protocols
- Environmental training materials
- Monthly and quarterly environmental reports

**Standards & Guidelines:**
- ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management Systems
- ISO 14046 Water Footprint
- ISO 14067 Greenhouse Gas Accounting
- Construction Environmental Management Plans (CEMPs)
- Client-specific environmental requirements

**Estimated Hours:** 200-400 hours per project

**Business Criticality:** High - Operational environmental control and compliance
