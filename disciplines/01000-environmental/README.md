# 01000 - Environmental

The Environmental discipline covers environmental management, sustainability, regulatory compliance, and ecological protection for construction and engineering projects.

## Overview

This discipline ensures environmental impact is minimized, sustainability targets are met, and all environmental regulations are complied with throughout project lifecycles.

## Key Responsibilities

- Environmental impact assessment
- Sustainability planning and reporting
- Regulatory compliance management
- Waste management and recycling
- Energy efficiency initiatives
- Environmental monitoring
- Ecological protection measures
- Climate risk assessment
