# Architectural Services Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Architectural services procurement covers the professional services of registered architects across all design stages from concept through construction completion. Services include design development, technical detailing, construction documentation, site inspections, contract administration, and specialist architectural consultancy. Procurement involves materials for mock-ups, presentation materials, sample boards, and technical reference supplies in support of architectural deliverables.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Materials & Samples | Finishes, fixtures, fittings samples; material libraries | Sample size per manufacturer standards, color/finish consistency | 1-3 weeks |
| Mock-Up Materials | Full-size wall sections, facade panels, joinery mock-ups | Match construction specifications, approved by design team | 4-8 weeks |
| Presentation Materials | Exhibition boards, presentation panels, portfolio materials | Archival quality, color-calibrated prints, mounting standards | 1-2 weeks |
| Technical Reference | Building codes, standards documents, technical guides | Latest edition compliance, SANS 10400, NHBRC, local bylaws | 1-2 weeks |
| Survey Equipment | Laser distance meters, levels, inspection cameras | Leica/Trimble accuracy specs, calibration certificates | 1-3 weeks |
| Office Supplies | Plotter paper, ink cartridges, drafting supplies | Media profile compatibility, archival ink standards | 1-2 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| Sample Procurement | Sample delivery, color matching, finish consistency | Approved sample sign-off, mock-up panel approval |
| Mock-Up Construction | Fabrication, installation, inspection | Mock-up review meeting, approved record sign-off |
| Presentation Production | Print quality, mounting, delivery | Color calibration check, deadline compliance |
| Material Library | Catalog updates, sample replenishment | Current product availability, superseded material removal |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Concept Design | Site analysis, design brief, concept options, feasibility | RIBA Stage 1/2 deliverables, SACAP registration |
| Detailed Design | Technical design, specification writing, tender documentation | RIBA Stage 3/4 deliverables, building regulation compliance |
| Construction Documentation | Working drawings, schedules, bills of quantities | Coordinated with engineers, local authority approval compliance |
| Contract Administration | Tender evaluation, progress monitoring, payment certification | FIDIC/NEC/JBCC contract familiarity, professional indemnity cover |
| Site Inspection | Progress inspections, quality checks, snag identification | SACAP site supervision guidelines, H&S file contribution |
| Specialist Consultancy | Heritage, sustainability, accessibility, fire engineering | Specialist registration / accreditation, peer review requirements |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Architectural Services

### Goods (PO Items)
- Material samples and sample boards with approved finish documentation
- Mock-up panels and full-size construction mock-ups for design verification
- Presentation materials and competition boards with archival quality requirements
- Technical reference materials with current edition compliance

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Design services at all RIBA stages with professional indemnity insurance
- Contract administration services with payment certification authority
- Site inspection services with SNCRC / SACAP registration requirements
- Specialist consultancy with discipline-specific accreditation and PI cover

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **RIBA Plan of Work:** Stage-specific scope definitions and procurement milestones (Stages 0-7)
- **SACAP Registration:** Mandatory registration for architectural services, code of conduct compliance
- **Professional Indemnity:** Minimum PI cover requirements (typically R1-10M depending on project scale)
- **Design Team Coordination:** Interface with structural, civil, electrical, mechanical engineers
- **Local Authority Approvals:** Plan submission, building control sign-off, occupancy certificate
- **Heritage Projects:** Heritage Impact Assessments (HIA), Heritage Western Cape / South African Heritage Resources Agency approvals
- **Green Building:** Green Star / LEED / EDGE accreditation, energy model certification, commissioning
