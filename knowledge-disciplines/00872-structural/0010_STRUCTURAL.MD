# Structural Engineering Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Structural engineering procurement covers steel structures, reinforced/prestressed concrete structures, foundations, structural steelwork, and structural strengthening for buildings, bridges, and industrial facilities. Procurement involves structural materials with strict SANS/ASTM/EN compliance, fabrication quality standards, and specialist design and inspection services.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Structural Steel | Beams, columns, bracing, trusses, handrails | SANS 10163 / EN 10025, steel grade S355/S275, paint spec | 8-20 weeks |
| Reinforcement | Rebar, welded mesh, threaded bars, couplers | SANS 920, grade 250/450/500, bend/diameter spec | 4-10 weeks |
| Bearings | Elastomeric, pot, spherical, roller bearings | EN 1337 / SANS 10160, vertical load, rotation capacity | 8-16 weeks |
| Expansion Joints | Modular, strip seal, finger plate, compression seal | SANS 10160, movement capacity, installation component | 6-12 weeks |
| Fixings & Anchors | Holding-down bolts, chemical anchors, expansion anchors | SANS 10160, tension/shear capacity, corrosion protection | 2-4 weeks |
| Precast Elements | Precast beams, columns, panels, stair flights, planks | SANS 10160, lifting insert capacity, finish standard | 8-16 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| Steel Fabrication | Material procurement, cutting, welding, trial erection | Material certs, weld map, dimensional check, NDE reports |
| Precast Production | Mould preparation, reinforcement cage, casting, curing | Dimensional check, cover meter, lifting insert pull test |
| Bearing Supply | Elastomer compounding, reinforcement lamination, load test | Compressive modulus test, bond test, rotation test |
| Stud/Bolt Supply | Material certification, thread quality, batch marking | Tensile test, hardness test, coating thickness check |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Structural Design | Load analysis, structural modeling, member design | SANS 10160, Eurocode 3/2, limit state design, BIM coordination |
| Connection Design | Bolted/welded connection design, base plate design | SANS 10160, design for fabrication/erection, CBFEM analysis |
| Steel Erection | Lifting, alignment, bolting, welding on site | Erection method statement, torque/tension records, safety plan |
| Inspection & NDE | Visual inspection, UT, MPI, DPI, radiographic testing | ISO 9712 / ASNT certification, NDE procedure, hold points |
| Strengthening Works | Carbon fibre wrapping, steel plate bonding, concrete jacketing | ICRI guidelines, epoxy specification, pull-off testing |
| Structural Monitoring | Deflection monitoring, vibration monitoring, crack gauging | Baseline survey, monitoring frequency, alarm thresholds |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Structural Engineering

### Goods (PO Items)
- Structural steel with material certification, NDE reports, and paint specification compliance
- Reinforcement steel with mill certificates and bending schedule compliance
- Bearings with design load certification and rotation capacity testing
- Precast elements with dimensional verification and lifting insert certification

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Structural design with limit state code compliance and BIM coordination
- Steel erection with bolting torque specification and weld quality records
- NDE inspection with certified personnel and approved procedures
- Strengthening works with epoxy specification and bond testing verification

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **High-Rise:** Lateral load design (wind/seismic), column shortening, floor vibration analysis
- **Industrial:** Crane girders, heavy duty flooring, dynamic loading consideration
- **Bridges:** Fatigue design, bearing replacement strategy, expansion joint selection
- **Foundations:** Pile group design, raft foundation, ground improvement interaction
- **Structural Steel:** Cambering, connection detailing, corrosion protection system
- **Concrete:** Shrinkage/creep, thermal cracking control, post-tensioning system design
