# Environmental Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Environmental procurement covers environmental impact assessments (EIA), environmental monitoring, pollution control, remediation, and rehabilitation services for industrial, mining, and infrastructure projects. Procurement involves monitoring equipment, sampling apparatus, remediation materials, and specialist environmental services with strict regulatory compliance.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Monitoring Equipment | Air quality monitors, water samplers, noise meters, weather stations | SANS 1929, SANS 10366, calibration cert, data logging capability | 4-10 weeks |
| Sampling Apparatus | Bottles, preservation kits, filters, sample coolers | Chain-of-custody compliant, sample volume, preservation method | 1-2 weeks |
| Remediation Materials | Absorbents, bioremediation agents, limestone, activated carbon | Waste type specific, remediation target, material safety data sheet | 2-4 weeks |
| Spill Response Kits | Spill kits, containment booms, absorbent pads, PPE | Type P/A/B/H, sorbent capacity, disposal procedure | 1-2 weeks |
| Filtration Systems | Dust suppression equipment, water treatment units, sediment traps | Emission/Fluid discharge spec, flow rate, filtration efficiency | 6-12 weeks |
| Signage & Barriers | Environmental signage, fencing, erosion control matting | Material spec, UV stability, installation requirements | 1-3 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| Monitoring Equipment | Delivery, calibration, deployment | Calibration certificate, baseline data logger setup |
| Remediation Materials | Stockpile, quality verification, application method | Material analysis cert, application rate verification |
| Filtration Systems | Manufacture, delivery, installation certification | Efficiency test report, installation inspection |
| Sampling Supplies | Batch quality, expiry, chain-of-custody forms | Batch number traceability, sample hold time, preservation check |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| EIA Studies | Baseline studies, impact assessment, EMP development | NEMA compliance, EIA regulations, BAR/EIR classification |
| Monitoring Programs | Air, water, noise, biodiversity monitoring | Monitoring protocol, SANS reference methods, SANAS lab accreditation |
| Remediation | Contaminated land remediation, groundwater treatment | Waste management license, remediation target levels, closure plan |
| Rehabilitation | Mine closure, wetland rehabilitation, topsoil management | Closure costing, rehabilitation plan, aftercare monitoring |
| Environmental Compliance | Auditing, reporting, EIA conditions tracking | ISO 14001, audit protocol, regulatory reporting deadlines |
| Ecological Services | Biodiversity assessment, wetland delineation, species surveys | SAMBI/MBA protocols, biodiversity offset requirements, permit conditions |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Environmental

### Goods (PO Items)
- Monitoring equipment with calibration certification and data logging specification
- Sampling apparatus with chain-of-custody and preservation method compliance
- Remediation materials with waste-type suitability and safety data sheet provision
- Spill response equipment with type classification and disposal procedure

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- EIA studies with regulatory compliance and public participation requirements
- Environmental monitoring with SANS reference method and accredited laboratory support
- Remediation with waste management licensing and target level verification
- Rehabilitation with closure planning and aftercare monitoring commitments

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **EIA Process:** Scoping, impact assessment, public participation, environmental authorization
- **Water Use Licensing:** Integrated water use license (IWUL), stream diversion, groundwater abstraction
- **Air Quality:** RMAQF compliance, dispersion modeling, dust fallout monitoring
- **Biodiversity:** Protected species survey, biodiversity offset, rehabilitation planning
- **Waste Management:** Waste classification, disposal site licensing, recycling targets
- **Mine Closure:** Rehabilitation cost estimation, final landform design, post-closure monitoring period
