# EPC Contracts Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
EPC (engineering, procurement, and construction) contracts encompass lump-sum turnkey (LSTK), reimbursable, and target-price delivery models for large-scale industrial and infrastructure projects. Procurement involves long-lead equipment, bulk materials, subcontract packages, and integrated engineering and construction services spanning the full project lifecycle.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Long-Lead Equipment | Compressors, turbines, pressure vessels, heat exchangers | ASME/ASTM, OEM specifications, NACE MR0175 | 30-60 weeks |
| Bulk Materials | Piping, valves, fittings, flanges, structural steel | ASTM/ASME material specs, pressure ratings, corrosion allowance | 8-20 weeks |
| Electrical Equipment | MV/LV switchgear, transformers, MCCs, cables | IEC 62271, SANS 10142, hazardous area classification | 16-30 weeks |
| Instrumentation | DCS/PLC systems, analyzers, control valves | MESC specs, SIL ratings, loop check requirements | 12-24 weeks |
| Civil Materials | Cement, reinforcement, aggregates, formwork | SANS 50197, SANS 920, SANS 1083 structural specs | 4-12 weeks |
| Subcontract Packages | Modular skids, packaged units, prefabricated components | OEM interface specifications, module transport limits | 20-40 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| Long-Lead Equipment | Manufacturing milestones, material procurement | NDE reports, material certificates, inspection hold points |
| Bulk Materials | Mill release, fabrication progress | Mill test certificates, coating inspection, dimensional checks |
| Subcontract Packages | Design approval, fabrication, FAT | Design gate approvals, FAT attendance, shipping readiness |
| Engineered Equipment | Engineering deliverables, procurement status | Data sheet approvals, vendor document approvals (VDAs) |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Engineering Services | FEED, detail design, 3D modeling, value engineering | Design codes (ASME, API, IEC), independent design verification |
| Project Management | Planning, cost control, risk management, reporting | PMBOK, earned value management (EVM), contract specifications |
| Construction | Site preparation, concrete, steel erection, piping | Construction safety plan, ITPs, weld quality documentation |
| Commissioning | Pre-commissioning, commissioning, startup, performance test | Commissioning procedures, performance guarantees, handover dossiers |
| Quality Assurance | Inspection, NDE, auditing, documentation | ISO 9001, project QCP, NDE procedures per ASNT |
| Logistics | Freight forwarding, customs clearance, site delivery | Incoterms 2020, dangerous goods regulations, transport permits |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for EPC Contracts

### Goods (PO Items)
- Long-lead equipment with strict manufacturing QA/QC and inspection hold points
- Bulk materials with material traceability and mill test certification
- Subcontract equipment packages with interface control documents
- Spare parts and commissioning spares as defined in contract

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Engineering services with design milestones and gate approvals
- Construction services with quality ITP sign-off and safety compliance
- Commissioning services with performance testing against contractual guarantees
- Project management services with schedule and cost performance metrics

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **LSTK (Lump-Sum Turnkey):** Fixed price with owner scope definability; procurement risk held by contractor; strict change management
- **Reimbursable (Cost-Plus):** Owner bears procurement cost risk; open-book pricing with audit rights; incentive fee structures
- **Target Price:** Shared risk/reward; procurement savings sharing; rigorous target cost auditing
- **EPCM (Engineering, Procurement, Construction Management):** Contractor manages procurement on owner's behalf; no construction risk; professional services model
