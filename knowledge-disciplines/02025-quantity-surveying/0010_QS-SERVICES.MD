# Quantity Surveying Services Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Quantity surveying procurement covers cost estimation, bill of quantities (BOQ) preparation, cost planning, contract administration, valuation, and claims assessment for construction, infrastructure, and industrial projects. Procurement involves specialized software, measurement tools, cost databases, and professional quantity surveying services with standards compliance.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| QS Software | Cost estimating, BOQ, contract admin, project costing | Integration with CAD/BIM, elemental cost analysis, reporting | 2-4 weeks |
| Measurement Tools | Digital take-off tablets, measuring wheels, distometers | Area/volume calculation, CAD integration, accuracy spec | 1-3 weeks |
| Cost Databases | Historical cost data, price books, market intelligence | Regional applicability, update frequency, trade breakdown | 1-4 weeks |
| Estimating Manuals | Standard costing references, SMM guides, prelims templates | Latest edition, SMM 7 / NRM / CESMM compliance | 1-2 weeks |
| Office Equipment | Scanners, copiers, plotters, filing systems | A3/A0 scanning, archival storage, document management | 2-4 weeks |
| Reference Materials | Contract documentation, case law, professional standards | RICS QS standards, SACQSP guidance, latest contract editions | 1-2 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| Software Licenses | License activation, user training, update subscription | License key receipt, training completion, version currency |
| Cost Database | Database subscription, data import, user access | Data release schedule, regional module activation |
| Measurement Tools | Calibration, delivery inspection, user assignment | Calibration certificate, check measurement, condition survey |
| Subscription Renewals | Renewal date, terms negotiation, continuous coverage | Automatic renewal terms, enterprise agreement, multi-year pricing |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Cost Estimation | Order of magnitude, budget estimate, detailed estimate | AACE classification (Class 1-5), accuracy tolerance, WBS alignment |
| BOQ Preparation | Measurement, take-off, schedule preparation | SMM 7 / NRM 1-3 / CESMM, measurement rules compliance |
| Procurement Support | Tender documentation, pricing schedule, evaluation | PPPFA/NFPA compliance, tender pricing analysis |
| Valuation | Progress valuation, payment certificate, final account | Contract measurement rules, unit rate application, retention |
| Claims Assessment | Delay/ disruption analysis, quantum assessment, EOT evaluation | SCL Protocol, forensic delay analysis, contemporaneous records |
| Cost Management | Cost reporting, cash flow forecasting, variance analysis | EVM (earned value management), cost code structure, audit trail |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Quantity Surveying

### Goods (PO Items)
- QS software with cost estimating, BOQ, and contract administration functionality
- Measurement tools with area/volume calculation accuracy and CAD integration
- Cost databases with regional applicability and regular update subscriptions
- Professional reference materials with codes of measurement compliance

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Cost estimation with AACE classification accuracy targets and WBS alignment
- BOQ preparation with standard method of measurement compliance
- Valuation and payment certification with contract term application
- Claims assessment with forensic analysis protocol and contemporaneous record review

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **Standard Method of Measurement:** SMM7, NRM 1-3, CESMM, or project-specific measurement rules
- **Cost Classification:** AACE Class 1-5 estimates with defined accuracy ranges and end-use purpose
- **Contract Administration:** Monitoring cost, change management, payment certification, final account
- **BIM Integration:** 5D cost modeling, automated quantity extraction, model-based measurement
- **Risk Management:** Cost contingency analysis, risk register, Monte Carlo simulation
- **Life Cycle Costing:** Whole life cost analysis, LCC model, operational expenditure forecasting
