# Landscaping Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Landscaping procurement covers plants, irrigation systems, hardscaping elements, and softscaping for commercial, residential, and public realm projects. Procurement involves living materials with establishment guarantees, irrigation components with water efficiency requirements, and hardscape materials with durability and aesthetic specifications.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Trees & Shrubs | Specimen trees, hedging, ornamental shrubs, ground cover | SANS 10163, container size, height/girth spec, root ball | 4-16 weeks |
| Turf & Grass | Rolled turf, hydroseed, grass seed mixes | Species mix, purity, germination rate, weed-free cert | 1-4 weeks |
| Irrigation Components | Pipes, drippers, sprinklers, controllers, valves | Water efficiency rating, pressure rating, backflow prevention | 2-6 weeks |
| Paving & Hardscaping | Pavers, kerbstones, retaining wall blocks, stepping stones | Compressive strength, slip resistance, colour consistency | 4-8 weeks |
| Landscape Lighting | LED fixtures, transformers, control systems | IP65/67 rating, lumens output, colour temperature | 2-6 weeks |
| Soil & Mulch | Topsoil, compost, bark mulch, decorative stone | SANS 10163 soil spec, organic content, particle size | 1-3 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| Plant Material | Nursery sourcing, size verification, disease inspection | Plant health certificate, size/grade verification, quarantine |
| Irrigation System | Component delivery, pressure test, controller programming | Pressure test report, coverage test, rain sensor integration |
| Hardscape Materials | Colour match, dimensional check, delivery schedule | Sample approval, batch consistency, pallet delivery condition |
| Soil & Growing Medium | Testing, certification, delivery | Soil analysis report, organic content, pH verification |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Planting | Tree planting, shrub installation, turf laying, hydroseeding | Planting pit spec, staking/guying, watering schedule, establishment period |
| Irrigation Installation | Trenching, pipe laying, sprinkler installation, controller setup | Backflow prevention, pressure test, coverage uniformity test |
| Landscape Maintenance | Mowing, pruning, fertilizing, weed control, pest management | Maintenance schedule, chemical application records, H&S compliance |
| Hardscape Construction | Paving, retaining walls, steps, water features | Sub-base compaction, jointing, drainage provision, slip resistance |
| Softscape Preparation | Soil preparation, grading, mulching, composting | Soil amendment spec, grading tolerance, mulch depth |
| Tree Management | Tree survey, pruning, removal, transplanting | Arborist certification, protected species compliance, method statement |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Landscaping

### Goods (PO Items)
- Plant material with nursery certification, size/grade verification, and establishment guarantee
- Irrigation components with water efficiency rating and backflow prevention certification
- Hardscape materials with colour consistency, slip resistance, and compressive strength testing
- Soil and growing medium with laboratory analysis and organic content certification

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Planting services with establishment period maintenance and replacement guarantee
- Irrigation installation with pressure testing and coverage verification
- Landscape maintenance with scheduled service levels and chemical application records
- Hardscape construction with sub-base compaction and drainage compliance

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **Establishment Period:** Plant replacement guarantee (typically 6-12 months), watering schedule, survival rate
- **Irrigation Efficiency:** Water use restrictions, rainwater harvesting integration, smart controller programming
- **Indigenous Landscaping:** Indigenous species requirement, water-wise planting, biodiversity enhancement
- **Public Realm:** Durability specifications, anti-vandalism measures, accessibility compliance
- **Green Roofs:** Substrate depth, drainage layer, plant selection for exposure, irrigation access
- **Rehabilitation:** Topsoil management, erosion control, invasive species removal, revegetation plan
