# Energy & Power Project Typology — Geotechnical Engineering Perspective

## Discipline Overview for Energy & Power

This document provides the **Geotechnical Engineering** perspective on energy & power projects. It covers discipline-specific considerations, deliverables, compliance requirements, and workflows relevant to geotechnical investigations, soil mechanics, foundation design, slope stability, ground improvement, and site characterization.
