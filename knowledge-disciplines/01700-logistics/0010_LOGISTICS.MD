# Logistics Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Logistics procurement covers transport services, warehousing, freight forwarding, fleet management, materials handling, and supply chain coordination for construction and industrial projects. Procurement involves vehicles, fuel, containers, tracking systems, and comprehensive logistics services with regulatory compliance, dangerous goods handling, and site logistics planning requirements.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Vehicles | Trucks, trailers, light vehicles, site buses | GVW, payload capacity, fuel type, emissions standard | 8-20 weeks |
| Fuel & Lubricants | Diesel, petrol, lubricants, AdBlue, greases | SANS 342, EN 590 diesel spec, storage and dispensing | 1-2 weeks |
| Containers | Shipping containers, flat racks, tanks, reefers | ISO 668, CSC plate, container condition grade | 2-6 weeks |
| Pallets & Packaging | Pallets, crates, stillages, dunnage, shrink wrap | ISPM 15 pallet heat treatment, load capacity, materials spec | 1-3 weeks |
| Tracking Systems | GPS trackers, fleet management systems, telemetry | Real-time tracking, geofencing, integration with project system | 2-6 weeks |
| Materials Handling | Forklifts, pallet jacks, cranes, lifting beams, slings | Load capacity, lift height, certification, inspection interval | 4-12 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| Vehicle Procurement | Manufacturing, inspection, registration | Vehicle inspection cert, license registration, GPS installation |
| Fuel Supply | Delivery schedule, quality testing, storage insp | Fuel analysis cert, dip test, delivery docket reconciliation |
| Container Leasing | Container delivery, condition survey, return schedule | Condition survey report, lease term, demurrage tracking |
| Fleet Tracking | Device installation, system integration, test | Device activation, geofence setup, data feed verification |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Transport Services | Bulk haulage, flatbed, low-bed, tanker, tipper | Transport permits (abnormal loads), driver hours compliance, load restraint |
| Warehousing | Storage, inventory management, order picking, kitting | WMS system, FIFO/FEFO rotation, inventory accuracy target |
| Freight Forwarding | Sea, air, road, rail freight; customs clearance | Incoterms 2020, customs broker license, dangerous goods doc |
| Customs Clearance | Import/export clearance, duty classification, rebate claims | SARS customs registration, tariff classification, duty savings |
| Fleet Maintenance | Preventive maintenance, repairs, tyre management, licensing | OEM maintenance schedule, roadworthy compliance, license renewal |
| Site Logistics | Material receiving, laydown yard management,site transport | Site logistics plan, delivery scheduling, laydown area allocation |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Logistics

### Goods (PO Items)
- Vehicles with GVW/payload specification, emissions compliance, and registration requirements
- Fuel and lubricants with SANS spec and quality testing certifications
- Shipping containers with ISO standard, CSC plate, and condition survey
- Tracking systems with real-time monitoring and project system integration capability

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Transport services with permits for abnormal loads and driver hours compliance
- Warehousing with WMS and inventory accuracy SLA targets
- Freight forwarding with Incoterms application and customs compliance
- Fleet maintenance with OEM schedule compliance and roadworthy management

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **Abnormal Loads:** Route survey, permit applications, escort requirements, road closure coordination
- **Remote Sites:** Dedicated fleet, fuel storage and dispensing, camp logistics, airstrip coordination
- **Just-in-time Delivery:** Delivery windows, unloading coordination, queuing management, demurrage avoidance
- **Inventory Management:** Critical spares classification, min/max levels, consignment stock, MRP integration
- **Cross-Border Logistics:** Customs documentation, country-specific permits, transit bonds, temporary importation
- **Dangerous Goods:** ADR/IMDG/IATA classification, driver training, vehicle equipment, emergency response plan
