# Civil Works Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Civil engineering procurement covers earthworks, concrete structures, road construction, drainage systems, and bulk infrastructure for industrial, commercial, and residential projects. Procurement involves bulk construction materials, formwork systems, reinforcement steel, and specialized civil construction services with strict compaction and concrete testing requirements.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Cement & Binders | Portland cement, blended cements, fly ash | SANS 50197-1, strength class 32.5/42.5/52.5 | 1-4 weeks |
| Aggregates | Crushed stone, sand, gravel, fill material | SANS 1083 grading, LA abrasion, flakiness index | 1-2 weeks |
| Reinforcement Steel | Rebar, mesh, prestressing strand | SANS 920, SANS 924, grade 250/450/500 | 2-6 weeks |
| Formwork | Plywood, modular formwork, props, scaffolding | SANS 10160 load design, formwork classification | 2-6 weeks |
| Drainage Materials | Pipes (concrete, HDPE, PVC), catch basins, geotextiles | SANS 1200 standard, pipe class/strength rating | 2-4 weeks |
| Road Materials | Asphalt, bitumen, road base, kerbing | SANS 4001, penetration grade, compaction spec | 2-4 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| Concrete Supply | Batching, delivery, strength testing | Cube test results (7/28 day), slump test, delivery dockets |
| Reinforcement | Fabrication, delivery, bending schedule | Mill certs, bending schedule approval, cover spacers |
| Precast Elements | Mould preparation, casting, curing | Dimensional check, concrete strength, lifting insert test |
| Asphalt Plant | Mix design, production, delivery | Marshall stability, binder content, compaction temperature |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Bulk Earthworks | Site clearing, cut/fill, compaction, levelling | Compaction testing (MOD AASHTO), survey verification |
| Concrete Placement | Foundation slabs, columns, beams, retaining walls | Concrete spec SANS 1200, falsework design, curing |
| Road Construction | Sub-base, base course, asphalt wearing course | Layer works specifications, compaction, camber/superelevation |
| Piling | Bored piles, driven piles, CFA piles, sheet piling | PDA testing, pile integrity testing, load test verification |
| Drainage Installation | Stormwater pipes, culverts, attenuation structures | CCTV inspection, hydraulic capacity test, compaction as-built |
| Pavement Rehabilitation | Milling, overlay, crack sealing, surface dressing | Bond test, texture depth, skid resistance test |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Civil Works

### Goods (PO Items)
- Bulk construction materials with batch certification and material test reports
- Reinforcement steel with mill certificates and bending schedule compliance
- Precast concrete elements with dimensional and strength verification
- Drainage pipes with class rating and leak-tightness certification

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Earthworks with compaction testing and survey control requirements
- Concrete placement with falsework design and curing procedures
- Road construction with layer works specifications and density testing
- Piling with PDA/integrity testing and load test verification

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **Bulk Earthworks:** Cut/fill optimization, material classification (rock vs earth), compaction standards
- **Roads & Pavements:** Traffic classification, CBR design, layer thickness specification
- **Concrete Works:** Mix design approval, curing methods, formwork striking times
- **Drainage:** Hydrological analysis, pipe capacity design, attenuation/storage requirements
- **Retaining Structures:** Geotechnical design, drainage provision, waterproofing requirements
- **Bridges & Culverts:** Structural design, bearing selection, expansion joint specification
