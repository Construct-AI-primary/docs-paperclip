# Health & Hygiene Project Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Occupational health and hygiene procurement covers monitoring equipment, sampling kits, treatment supplies, clinic equipment, and professional health services. Procurement must comply with occupational health regulations, exposure limits, and medical confidentiality standards.

## Common PO Types

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Monitoring Equipment | Sound level meters, dust monitors, gas detectors, weather stations | Measurement range, data logging, calibration cert, ATEX rating | 2-6 weeks |
| Sampling Kits | Air sampling pumps, filter cassettes, passive samplers, swab kits | Flow rate, collection media, analysis compatibility | 1-3 weeks |
| Treatment Supplies | First aid consumables, eyewash solution, decontamination supplies | Expiry dates, ISO 13485, compatibility with materials | 1-2 weeks |
| Clinic Equipment | Audiometer, spirometer, vision tester, exam couch, autoclave | Calibration standards, test frequency, data storage, certification | 4-12 weeks |

## Common WO Types

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Medical Surveillance | Fit-to-work, audiometric, lung function, blood tests | OHSA/COIDA, confidentiality, accredited practitioner, records retention |
| Hygiene Monitoring | Personal exposure monitoring, noise surveys, dust sampling | NIOSH/OSHA methods, accredited laboratory analysis |
| Wellness Programs | EAP services, health screening, stress management | Professional registration, confidentiality agreements |
| Ergonomics | DSE assessment, manual handling assessment, workstation setup | Ergonomic standards, assessment documentation, follow-up recommendations |

## Key Distinctions
### Goods (PO Items)
- Monitoring equipment with calibration and certification requirements
- Sampling media with analysis compatibility and chain-of-custody
- Clinic equipment with medical device certification and maintenance schedules

### Services (WO Items)
- Medical surveillance with confidentiality, accredited professionals, and records retention
- Hygiene monitoring with accredited laboratories and method validation
- Wellness programs with professional registration and outcome tracking

## Project-Specific Considerations
- **Industrial Facilities:** Noise monitoring, respirable dust, chemical exposure assessment
- **Mining Operations:** Silica monitoring, noise surveys, functional capacity assessments
- **Healthcare Facilities:** Infection control, sharps management, latex allergy screening
- **Confined Spaces:** Atmospheric monitoring, personal gas detection, medical fitness certification
