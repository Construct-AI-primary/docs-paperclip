# Construction Safety Project Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Construction safety procurement covers personal protective equipment (PPE), safety equipment, fall protection systems, safety signage, fire extinguishers, first aid supplies, and safety training services. Procurement must comply with OSHA, local safety regulations, and project-specific safety plans.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| PPE (Personal Protective) | Hard hats, safety glasses, earplugs, gloves, safety boots | ANSI/CSA/EN standards, impact rating, comfort rating, size ranges | 1-4 weeks |
| Fall Protection | Full-body harnesses, lanyards, lifelines, anchor points, safety nets | ANSI Z359, max arrest force, max free fall, weight capacity | 2-6 weeks |
| Safety Signage | Warning signs, mandatory signs, exit signs, barrier tape | Size, material (vinyl/rigid), pictogram standards, photoluminescent | 1-3 weeks |
| Fire Extinguishers | ABC dry chemical, CO2, water, foam extinguishers | UL/ULC rating, capacity lbs/kg, type rating, rechargeable | 1-4 weeks |
| First Aid Supplies | First aid kits, AEDs, stretchers, eye wash stations | OSHA requirements, ANSI Z308.1 kit class, AED pads/electrodes | 1-3 weeks |
| Confined Space Equipment | Gas monitors, ventilation blowers, tripods, retrieval systems | LEL/O2/H2S sensors, airflow CFM, winch capacity, tripod height | 2-6 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| PPE Lot Deliveries | Bulk order, size distribution compliance | Size mix verification, standard certs, batch numbers |
| Fall Protection | Equipment certification, test dates | ANSI cert labels, inspection date, load test certs |
| Fire Equipment | Pressure gauges, rechargeable status | Pressure readings, hydrostatic test date, inspection tag |
| First Aid | Kit contents, expiry dates | Contents checklist, medication/supply expiry dates |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Safety Training | HSE induction, working at height, confined space, fire warden | Accredited trainer, certification validity, training records |
| Safety Auditing | Site safety audits, risk assessments, method statement review | ISO 45001, auditor certification, audit documentation |
| Medical Surveillance | Fit-to-work assessments, hearing tests, lung function tests | OHSA requirements, occupational health practitioner certs |
| Ergonomic Assessment | Workstation assessments, manual handling assessments | Ergonomic certification, assessment documentation, recommendations |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Construction Safety

### Goods (PO Items)
- PPE with expiry/inspection dates and replacement cycles
- Fall protection with certification and inspection requirements
- Emergency equipment with maintenance and inspection schedules

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Training with certification records and competency assessment
- Auditing with documented findings and corrective action tracking
- Medical surveillance with confidentiality and record-keeping requirements

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **High-Rise Construction:** Enhanced fall protection, perimeter netting, debris netting, material hoisting safety
- **Confined Space Work:** Gas monitoring, ventilation, rescue plan, standby personnel
- **Demolition:** Dust control, asbestos/HAZMAT handling, structural collapse prevention
- **Trenching/Excavation:** Shoring systems, cave-in protection, atmospheric monitoring
- **Electrical Work:** Arc flash PPE, rubber insulating gloves, hot stick tools, LOTO equipment
