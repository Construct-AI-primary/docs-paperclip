# Contract Administration Post-Award Typology

## Sector Overview
Post-award contract administration manages the execution phase of contracts including variation orders, claims management, progress payments, extension of time administration, performance monitoring, and contract close-out. Procurement involves documentation systems, amendment instruments, and specialist advisory services for dispute resolution and contract compliance.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Contract Amendments | Variation order packs, addenda documentation | Contract clause referencing, approval authority matrix | 1-3 weeks |
| Extension of Time Documentation | EOT claim packs, delay analysis reports | Forensic delay analysis protocols (SCL/SPA) | 2-6 weeks |
| Performance Securities | Performance bonds, retention bonds, advance payment guarantees | Bank guarantees per ICC 758, expiry alignment with defects liability | 1-3 weeks |
| Document Management Systems | CMS platforms, contract registers, correspondence logs | ISO 9001 compliant, audit trail, version controlled | 4-8 weeks |
| Insurance Certificates | Construction all risks, PI insurance, CAR insurance | Policy coverage minimums, insurer ratings, notification periods | 1-3 weeks |
| Close-Out Documentation | Handover certificates, final account packs, as-built records | Contractual milestone sign-off, defects certificate | 4-12 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| Variation Orders | VO submission, approval, implementation | Cost approval within delegated authority, time impact assessment |
| Progress Payments | Payment applications, certification, release | Milestone achievement verification, retention release conditions |
| Claims Assessment | Notice of claim, substantiation, determination | Contractual notice periods, supporting documentation completeness |
| Defects Management | Defect notification, rectification, certificate | Defects liability period expiry, snag list clearance |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Contract Administration | Progress monitoring, correspondence management, site meetings | Contract administration manual, record keeping protocols |
| Dispute Resolution | Adjudication, mediation, arbitration, expert determination | Dispute resolution board (DRB) rules, adjudication timetables |
| Delay Analysis | Forensic delay analysis, productivity analysis, EOT assessment | SCL Delay & Disruption Protocol, AACE recommended practices |
| Quantity Surveying | Measurement, valuation, cost reporting, final account | Standard method of measurement, contract rates and prices |
| Legal Advisory | Contract interpretation, claims assessment, litigation support | Legal privilege, confidentiality, conflict of interest |
| Close-Out Services | Final account agreement, defects management, lessons learned | Contractual close-out checklist, warranties and guarantees register |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Contract Administration

### Goods (PO Items)
- Variation order and amendment documentation with formal approval chain
- Performance securities and bonds with strict expiry tracking
- Document management systems with full audit trail capability
- Close-out and handover certificates as formal contractual instruments

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Contract administration with documented procedures and record keeping
- Dispute resolution services with formal procedural rules and timelines
- Delay analysis with recognized forensic protocols and expert testimony
- Quantity surveying with measurement standards compliance

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **FIDIC Contracts:** Engineer's determination procedures, claim notification timelines, DAB referral
- **NEC Contracts:** Early warning mechanisms, compensation events, project manager assessments
- **Government Contracts:** PFMA compliance, audit requirements, procurement regulation adherence
- **Joint Ventures:** Interface management, cost sharing agreements, lead contractor coordination
- **Subcontracts:** Back-to-back terms, pay-when-paid provisions, default notice procedures
- **Termination:** For convenience, for default, step-in rights, termination account preparation
