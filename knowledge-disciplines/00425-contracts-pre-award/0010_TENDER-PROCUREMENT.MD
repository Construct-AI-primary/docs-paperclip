# Tender & Procurement Pre-Award Typology

## Sector Overview
Pre-award procurement covers the entire tendering lifecycle including market engagement, expression of interest, invitation to tender (ITT) / request for proposal (RFP) preparation, bid evaluation, negotiation, and award recommendation. Procurement involves specialized documentation services, evaluation software, bid bonds, and technical-commercial advisory support.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| ITT/RFP Documentation | Tender packs, specification documents, drawings sets | ISO 9001 document control, version management | 2-4 weeks |
| Bid Bonds & Guarantees | Bid bonds, performance bonds, parent company guarantees | Bank instrument per ICC 758/URDG 758, expiry terms | 1-3 weeks |
| Evaluation Software | E-tendering platforms, bid evaluation tools, scoring systems | Cloud-based, audit trail, encrypted submission | 4-8 weeks |
| Advertising & Notices | Tender notices, public advertisements, trade publications | Procurement act compliance, mandatory publication platforms | 1-2 weeks |
| Confidentiality Agreements | NDA documentation, secure data rooms | Legal jurisdiction, data protection compliance | 1-2 weeks |
| Presentation Materials | Bid briefing packs, pre-bid meeting materials, addenda | Consistent format, formal distribution log | 1-2 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| Tender Documentation | Document delivery, addenda issue | Tender issue receipt, addenda acknowledgment |
| Bid Submissions | Bid receipt deadline, submission completeness | Bid box/tender portal timestamp, mandatory document checklist |
| Evaluation Process | Evaluation milestones, scoring consistency | Evaluation committee minutes, scoring sign-off, consensus records |
| Clarification Rounds | Query responses, clarification meetings | Formal response register, equal treatment compliance |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Market Engagement | Pre-qualification, market sounding, industry briefings | PPPFA procurement principles, transparency requirements |
| Technical Evaluation | Technical proposal assessment, methodology review | Tender evaluation criteria, weighted scoring model |
| Commercial Evaluation | Pricing analysis, cost benchmarking, TCO assessment | Pricing formula compliance, cost breakdown verification |
| Negotiation Support | Commercial negotiations, contract terms, MOUs | Negotiation mandate, BATNA analysis, negotiation record |
| Legal Review | Contract terms review, compliance assessment, risk allocation | Legal framework compliance, risk acceptance criteria |
| Bid Advisory | Bid strategy, proposal development, submission support | Conflict of interest declaration, confidentiality |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Tender & Procurement Pre-Award

### Goods (PO Items)
- Tender documentation packages with controlled distribution and issue tracking
- Bid bonds and guarantees with strict expiry date management
- E-tendering software with audit trail and data security requirements
- Advertising placements meeting mandatory publication requirements

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- Technical and commercial evaluation services with documented scoring
- Legal services with conflict of interest and confidentiality obligations
- Negotiation support with formal mandate and record keeping
- Market engagement services with transparency and equal treatment

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **Open Tendering:** Public advertising, unlimited participation, strict equal treatment
- **Restricted Tendering:** Pre-qualification stage, shortlisted bidders, competitive dialogue
- **Negotiated Procedure:** Direct negotiation justified, documented rationale, limited circumstances
- **Framework Agreements:** Multiple suppliers, call-off mechanisms, term period and pricing schedules
- **Design & Construct:** Alternative technical proposals, design development during evaluation
- **Public Sector:** PPPFA compliance, preferential procurement, local content requirements
