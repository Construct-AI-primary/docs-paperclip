# Sundry Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Sundry procurement covers general items, consumables, office supplies, temporary facilities, and miscellaneous goods and services that support construction, industrial, and site-based projects. While individually lower value, sundry procurement requires efficient ordering systems, bulk purchasing agreements, and inventory management to avoid project delays.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Office Supplies | Stationery, paper, toner, filing, printing consumables | Brand preference, bulk pack size, recycled content target | 1-2 weeks |
| Consumables | Batteries, tapes, adhesives, sealants, lubricants | Shelf life, packaging unit, material safety data sheet | 1-2 weeks |
| Safety Consumables | Gloves, safety glasses, ear plugs, dust masks | SANS standard, disposable/reusable, size range, box quantity | 1-2 weeks |
| Tools & Equipment | Hand tools, power tools, measuring, site inspection tools | Brand specification, durability rating, warranty, calibration | 1-4 weeks |
| Temporary Facilities | Site offices, toilets, containers, fencing, water tanks | SANS 10400, sanitation spec, fire rating, security requirement | 1-4 weeks |
| First Aid Supplies | First aid kits, bandages, antiseptics, stretchers | SANS 10400-S, OHSA compliance, kit contents schedule | 1-2 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| Consumable Stock | Reorder levels, batch expiry, stock rotation | Min/max stock check, batch number expiry tracking |
| Tool Calibration | Calibration schedule, tool condition, user issue | Calibration cert, calibration sticker, issue register |
| Temporary Facilities | Delivery, installation, condition survey | Condition survey, connection sign-off, rental term |
| Office Inventory | Stock levels, ordering frequency, budget tracking | Monthly inventory check, consumption rate analysis |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| General Maintenance | Repairs, plumbing, electrical, painting, carpentry | Trade qualification, H&S compliance, job completion cert |
| Cleaning Services | Site cleaning, office cleaning, window cleaning, deep clean | Cleaning schedule, materials safety data sheet, waste disposal |
| Waste Management | General waste, recycling, hazardous waste disposal | Waste manifest, disposal site license, recycling target |
| Catering Services | Site canteen, vending, water coolers, event catering | Food safety (SANS 10049), hygiene cert, allergen management |
| IT Support | Desktop support, printer setup, network, helpdesk | SLA response time, remote support, on-site support schedule |
| Document Management | Scanning, copying, archiving, shredding, storage | POPIA compliance, document retention policy, destruction cert |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Sundry

### Goods (PO Items)
- Office supplies with bulk packaging and recycled content specifications
- Consumables with shelf life tracking and material safety data sheet compliance
- Tools and equipment with calibration and warranty requirements
- Temporary facilities with SANS compliance and fire rating certification

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- General maintenance with trade qualification and safety compliance
- Cleaning services with defined schedules and MSDS records
- Waste management with legal disposal certification and recycling targets
- Catering services with food safety certification and hygiene compliance

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **Site Establishment:** Temporary office, ablution, canteen, first aid room setup
- **Consumable Management:** Reorder point system, bulk purchase agreements, just-in-time delivery
- **Waste Management:** Site waste management plan, waste stream segregation, skip hire, recycling
- **Tool Management:** Tool register, calibrated tool database, lost/damaged tool procedure
- **Safety Consumables:** PPE issue register, replacement schedule, size range maintenance
- **Remote Sites:** Bulk ordering for remote locations, long lead time consumables, site stores management
