# Mechanical Engineering Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Mechanical engineering procurement covers HVAC systems, plumbing and water supply, fire protection, materials handling, and mechanical installations for buildings and industrial facilities. Procurement involves specialized mechanical equipment with strict SANS/ASHRAE code compliance, energy efficiency requirements, and commissioning procedures.

## Common PO Types (Purchase Orders for Goods)

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Chillers | Air-cooled, water-cooled, centrifugal, screw chillers | ASHRAE 90.1, SANS 10400-XA, COP/EER rating | 12-24 weeks |
| Air Handling Units | AHUs, fan coil units, ducting, diffusers, VAV boxes | SANS 10400-XA, filter class (MERV/HEPA), fan efficiency | 8-16 weeks |
| Pumps | Centrifugal pumps, booster sets, circulation pumps | SANS 1517/1527, pump efficiency, NPSHr, material spec | 6-14 weeks |
| Fire Protection | Sprinklers, fire hydrants, hose reels, fire extinguishers | SANS 10400-T, SANS 1187, SABS/NFPA compliance | 6-12 weeks |
| Plumbing Fixtures | Water closets, basins, urinals, water heaters, valves | SANS 10252, water consumption rating, SABS marking | 4-8 weeks |
| Conveyors | Belt conveyors, screw conveyors, bucket elevators | CEMA standard, belt width/speed, drive power | 10-20 weeks |

## Common SO Tracking Requirements

| Category | SO Focus Areas | Critical SO Verification Points |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------------|
| HVAC Equipment | Manufacturing, refrigeration circuit test, controls integration | Factory test report, refrigerant leak test, control comms test |
| Fire Protection | Pipe threading, sprinkler head installation, pump test | Hydrostatic test, flow test, alarm activation test |
| Plumbing Systems | Pipework, fixture installation, water test | Pressure test, water quality test, backflow prevention test |
| Conveyor Systems | Frame fabrication, belt installation, drive alignment | Belt tension test, alignment check, load run test |

## Common WO Types (Work Orders for Services)

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| HVAC Design | Load calculation, duct design, plant sizing, BMS integration | SANS 10400-XA, ASHRAE, energy model verification |
| Piping Design | Pipe sizing, routing, support design, stress analysis | ASME B31.1/B31.9, SANS 1128, pipe support spacing |
| Equipment Installation | Lifting, positioning, alignment, connection | OEM installation procedure, commissioning checklist |
| Commissioning | TAB (testing, adjusting, balancing), BMS point-to-point | NEBB / AABC TAB certification, BMS functional test |
| Maintenance | Preventive maintenance, repair, overhaul | OEM maintenance schedule, SLA compliance, spares availability |
| Ductwork Fabrication | Duct manufacturing, insulation, leak testing | SMACNA standards, duct leakage class, insulation thickness |

## Key Goods vs Services Distinctions for Mechanical Engineering

### Goods (PO Items)
- HVAC equipment with energy efficiency rating and factory test certification
- Fire protection equipment with SABS/NFPA certification and flow test reports
- Plumbing fixtures with water consumption rating and backflow prevention certification
- Conveyor systems with CEMA standard compliance and drive power verification

### Services (WO/Service Order Items)
- HVAC design with energy model compliance and BMS integration specification
- TAB commissioning with NEBB/AABC certification and balancing reports
- Equipment installation with OEM procedure compliance and alignment verification
- Ductwork fabrication with SMACNA standard compliance and leakage testing

## Project-Specific Procurement Considerations
- **Central Plant:** Chiller-plant sequencing, cooling tower selection, condenser water system design
- **Ventilation:** Minimum fresh air requirements, extract systems, heat recovery ventilation
- **Fire Fighting:** Fire hydrant coverage, sprinkler hazard classification, fire pump sizing
- **Plumbing:** Hot water recirculation, rainwater harvesting, greywater systems
- **Materials Handling:** Conveyor capacity, belt specification, chute design, transfer point dust suppression
- **BMS Integration:** BAS architecture, DDC controls, metering and sub-metering requirements
