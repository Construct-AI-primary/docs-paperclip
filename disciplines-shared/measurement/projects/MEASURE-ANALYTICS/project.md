## Goals

1. Implement measurement optimization engine with AI-powered enhancements
2. Create predictive analytics for measurement quality and performance
3. Develop automated performance optimization and self-tuning capabilities
4. Enable real-time analytics dashboards and reporting
5. Provide advanced AI features for measurement intelligence

- **Process Automation**: Identify and automate manual measurement processes
- **Accuracy Enhancement**: AI-driven accuracy improvements and error correction
