# SOLAR-006: Environmental Impact & Permitting

## Issue Summary

Conduct comprehensive environmental impact assessment and permitting coordination for solar PV installations. This issue covers NEPA/SEPA compliance, wildlife protection measures, stormwater management, cultural resource protection, and all required environmental permits.

## Objectives

- Complete environmental impact assessments
- Obtain all required environmental permits
- Develop wildlife protection and mitigation plans
- Ensure stormwater management compliance
- Protect cultural and historical resources
- Coordinate with regulatory agencies

## Scope of Work

### 1. Environmental Impact Assessment
- NEPA/SEPA documentation and compliance
- Environmental baseline studies
- Impact prediction and evaluation
- Mitigation strategy development

### 2. Permitting Coordination
- Federal, state, and local permit identification
- Application preparation and submission
- Agency coordination and negotiations
- Permit compliance monitoring

### 3. Wildlife Protection
- Avian and terrestrial species surveys
- Habitat impact assessment
- Protection and mitigation measures
- Monitoring and adaptive management

### 4. Stormwater Management
- NPDES permit requirements
- SWPPP development and implementation
- Erosion and sediment control
- Post-construction stormwater management

## Deliverables

1. **Environmental Impact Statement/Assessment**
2. **Comprehensive Permitting Package**
3. **Wildlife Protection Plan**
4. **Stormwater Management Plan**
5. **Cultural Resource Protection Plan**
6. **Environmental Compliance Documentation**
