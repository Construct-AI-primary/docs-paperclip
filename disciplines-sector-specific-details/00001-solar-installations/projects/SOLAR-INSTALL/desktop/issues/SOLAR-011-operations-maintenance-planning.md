# SOLAR-011: Operations & Maintenance Planning

## Issue Summary

Develop comprehensive operations and maintenance plans for solar PV installations. This issue covers preventive maintenance schedules, performance monitoring systems, operations procedures, and long-term asset management strategies.

## Objectives

- Establish preventive maintenance programs
- Implement performance monitoring systems
- Develop operations procedures
- Create maintenance schedules and protocols
- Plan for long-term asset management

## Scope of Work

### 1. Operations Planning
- System operation procedures
- Performance monitoring requirements
- Data acquisition and analysis
- Emergency operations procedures

### 2. Maintenance Planning
- Preventive maintenance schedules
- Predictive maintenance programs
- Corrective maintenance procedures
- Spare parts and inventory management

### 3. Monitoring Systems
- Performance monitoring equipment
- Data collection and analysis
- Alarm and alert systems
- Remote monitoring capabilities

### 4. Asset Management
- Long-term performance tracking
- Warranty management
- Equipment replacement planning
- End-of-life planning

## Deliverables

1. **Operations and Maintenance Manual**
2. **Preventive Maintenance Schedule**
3. **Performance Monitoring Plan**
4. **Spare Parts Inventory Plan**
5. **Asset Management Strategy**
