# SOLAR-005: Safety & Compliance Requirements

## Issue Summary

Develop comprehensive safety planning and compliance requirements for solar PV installations. This issue encompasses hazard identification, risk assessment, OSHA/NFPA compliance, personal protective equipment specifications, emergency response planning, and safety training programs.

## Objectives

- Identify and assess all solar installation hazards
- Develop comprehensive safety management plans
- Ensure OSHA, NFPA, and NEC safety compliance
- Establish emergency response and rescue procedures
- Implement safety training and qualification programs

## Scope of Work

### 1. Hazard Identification and Risk Assessment

#### Electrical Hazards
- Direct current (DC) arc flash and shock hazards
- Alternating current (AC) step/touch potentials
- Stored energy in capacitors and batteries
- Induced voltages on metallic structures

#### Fall Hazards
- Working at heights on ground-mounted systems
- Roof edge exposure on building installations
- Ladder and scaffold safety
- Fall protection system requirements

#### Structural and Mechanical Hazards
- Heavy equipment operation and lifting
- Structural collapse during installation
- Moving machinery on tracking systems
- Pinch points and crush hazards

### 2. Regulatory Compliance

#### OSHA Standards
- **29 CFR 1926**: Construction safety requirements
- **29 CFR 1910**: General industry standards
- **Fall Protection**: 6-foot threshold for ground systems
- **Electrical Safety**: Lockout/tagout and arc flash protection

#### NFPA Standards
- **NFPA 70E**: Electrical safety in the workplace
- **NFPA 1**: Fire code requirements
- **NFPA 101**: Life safety code for occupied buildings

#### NEC Requirements
- **Article 690.12**: Rapid shutdown requirements
- **Article 690.41**: Equipment grounding
- **Article 690.5**: Ground fault protection

### 3. Safety Management Plans

#### Site-Specific Safety Plan
- Hazard identification and mitigation
- Emergency communication procedures
- Medical emergency response
- Severe weather contingency plans

#### Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
- Arc-rated clothing and face shields
- Insulated gloves and tools
- Fall protection harnesses and lanyards
- Head, eye, and foot protection

### 4. Emergency Response Planning

#### Medical Emergencies
- First aid and CPR trained personnel
- Automated external defibrillators (AEDs)
- Emergency medical service coordination
- Bloodborne pathogen exposure procedures

#### Fire Response
- Portable fire extinguishers (Class ABC)
- Fire suppression system integration
- Evacuation procedures and routes
- Fire department coordination

#### Rescue Operations
- Technical rescue team requirements
- Confined space rescue capabilities
- High-angle rescue procedures
- Helicopter evacuation protocols

## Deliverables

1. **Comprehensive Safety Plan**
2. **Hazard Identification Report**
3. **Risk Assessment Matrix**
4. **Emergency Response Procedures**
5. **PPE Specifications and Training**
6. **OSHA/NFPA Compliance Documentation**
