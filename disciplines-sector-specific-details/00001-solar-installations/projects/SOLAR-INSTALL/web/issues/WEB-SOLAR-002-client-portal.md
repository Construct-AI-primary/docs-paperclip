# WEB-SOLAR-002: Client Portal

## Issue Summary

Develop a web-based client portal for solar installation customers. This portal provides customers with energy production reports, savings tracking, warranty management, maintenance scheduling, and account management capabilities.

## Objectives

- Provide customer energy production visibility
- Track cost savings and ROI
- Manage warranties and contracts
- Enable maintenance scheduling
- Support customer communication
- Enable bill management

## Scope of Work

### 1. Energy Production Reports
- **Production Data**: Daily, monthly, yearly generation
- **Consumption Data**: Home energy usage
- **Net Metering**: Grid export tracking
- **Peer Comparison**: Compare to similar homes
- **Environmental Impact**: Carbon offset metrics

### 2. Savings Tracking
- **Cost Savings**: Monthly bill savings
- **ROI Tracking**: Return on investment progress
- **Rate Analysis**: Time-of-use optimization
- **Bill Projections**: Estimated future bills
- **Incentive Tracking**: Tax credits and rebates

### 3. Warranty Management
- **Warranty Status**: Active warranty tracking
- **Equipment Coverage**: Component warranty details
- **Claims Processing**: Warranty claim submission
- **Extended Warranties**: Purchase extended coverage
- **Documentation**: Warranty certificates

### 4. Maintenance Scheduling
- **Service Requests**: Submit maintenance requests
- **Scheduling**: Book service appointments
- **Service History**: View past maintenance
- **Preventive Maintenance**: Scheduled maintenance alerts
- **Emergency Support**: 24/7 support contact

### 5. Account Management
- **Profile Management**: Update account information
- **Payment History**: View payment records
- **Document Storage**: Store important documents
- **Communication Preferences**: Notification settings
- **Support Tickets**: Submit and track support requests

### 6. Portal Features
- **Single Sign-On**: Easy authentication
- **Mobile Responsive**: Responsive design
- **Multi-Property**: Manage multiple installations
- **Notifications**: Email and SMS alerts
- **Help Center**: Self-service support

## Deliverables

1. **Web Portal**: Responsive client portal
2. **Customer Dashboard**: Personalized dashboard
3. **Service Module**: Maintenance scheduling
4. **Document Management**: Secure document storage
5. **Notification System**: Email and SMS integration

## Success Criteria

- **User Adoption**: > 80% customer registration
- **Report Accuracy**: Accurate production data
- **Savings Tracking**: Precise cost calculations
- **Warranty Management**: Complete warranty tracking
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Positive portal feedback
