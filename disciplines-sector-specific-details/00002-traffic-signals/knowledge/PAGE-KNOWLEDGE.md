## 1. Electrical Traffic Signal Knowledge

### Core Topics
- Traffic signal power distribution systems
- Signal cabinet wiring and components
- Protection and coordination schemes
- Cable sizing and conduit installation
- Grounding and bonding requirements
- Utility interconnection requirements

### Key Standards
- **NEC Article 750**: Traffic Control Systems
- **IEEE 1547**: Interconnection and Interoperability
- **UL 891**: Dead-Front Switches
- **NEMA TS 2**: Traffic Controller Assemblies

### Critical Requirements
- Uninterruptible power supply (UPS) for signal backup
- Battery backup systems for power outages
- Coordination with utility protection schemes
- Surge protection for sensitive electronics

## Cross-Discipline Integration

### Primary Integration Points
1. **Electrical + ITS**: Power supply for controllers and detection systems
2. **Civil + Electrical**: Conduit installation and foundation grounding
3. **ITS + Safety**: Emergency vehicle preemption and work zone safety
4. **Environmental + Civil**: Stormwater management and erosion control
5. **QA/QC + All Disciplines**: Quality verification at each phase

### Workflow Dependencies
- Site assessment feeds into civil and electrical design
- Civil design enables electrical conduit installation
- ITS design depends on electrical power supply
- Safety planning integrates with all field operations
- QA/QC verifies all discipline deliverables

## Best Practices

### Project Planning
- Early coordination between disciplines
- Clear scope definition and responsibilities
- Realistic scheduling with buffer time
- Risk identification and mitigation planning

### Design Coordination
- Regular interdisciplinary design reviews
- Clash detection for underground utilities
- Shared coordinate systems and baselines
- Clear interface documentation

### Construction Management
- Daily coordination meetings
- Clear change management procedures
- Quality verification at each stage
- Safety monitoring and enforcement

### Operations and Maintenance
- Comprehensive O&M manual development
- Training for operations personnel
- Preventive maintenance scheduling
- Performance monitoring and optimization
