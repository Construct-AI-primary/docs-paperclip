# Traffic Signals Cross-Disciplinary Project

This project provides comprehensive workflow templates and implementation guidance for traffic signal infrastructure installations, coordinating across multiple engineering disciplines to ensure safe, compliant, and efficient traffic control systems.

## Project Scope

- **Cross-Disciplinary Coordination**: Integration across electrical, civil, geotechnical, ITS (Intelligent Transportation Systems), safety, environmental, QA/QC, and security disciplines
- **Full Lifecycle Coverage**: From site assessment and feasibility through design, construction, commissioning, operations, and maintenance
- **Multi-Platform Implementation**: Desktop engineering tools, mobile field applications, and web monitoring portals
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Adherence to electrical codes, traffic engineering standards, environmental regulations, and safety requirements

## Key Objectives

1. **Standardized Traffic Signal Workflows**: Develop comprehensive, discipline-integrated workflows for traffic signal installations
2. **Knowledge Integration**: Compile and organize traffic signal-specific knowledge across all relevant engineering disciplines
3. **Quality Assurance**: Ensure compliance with industry standards and regulatory requirements
4. **Operational Excellence**: Enable efficient traffic signal project execution from concept to completion

## Target Users

- **Traffic Engineers**: Signal design and timing optimization
- **Civil Engineers**: Foundation and roadway design
- **Electrical Engineers**: Power distribution and signal electrical
- **ITS Specialists**: Controller programming and traffic management
- **Construction Contractors**: Installation procedures and quality control
- **Operations Teams**: Maintenance planning and performance monitoring
- **Municipalities**: Traffic management and compliance
