# TRAFFIC-005: Safety & Compliance Requirements

## Issue Summary

Develop comprehensive safety plans and compliance documentation for traffic signal installations. This phase ensures worker and public safety through proper work zone traffic control, electrical safety procedures, fall protection planning, and regulatory compliance throughout construction and maintenance.

## Objectives

- Develop work zone traffic control plans
- Create electrical safety procedures
- Design fall protection plans for pole installation
- Ensure OSHA and NEC compliance
- Prepare emergency response procedures
- Document all safety requirements

## Scope of Work

### 1. Work Zone Traffic Control

#### Traffic Control Plan Development
- **MUTCD Part 6**: Temporary traffic control compliance
- **Site-Specific Plans**: Plan for each work location
- **Agency Approval**: Approval from transportation agency
- **Implementation**: Proper setup and maintenance

#### Work Zone Components
- **Advance Warning Area**: Warning signs placement
- **Transition Area**: Taper calculations and devices
- **Activity Area**: Work space and buffer space
- **Termination Area**: Return to normal traffic

### 2. Electrical Safety Procedures

#### Arc Flash Hazard Analysis
- **IEEE 1584 Calculations**: Incident energy analysis
- **PPE Requirements**: Arc-rated clothing per hazard level
- **Warning Labels**: Labels on all enclosures
- **Boundaries**: Limited, restricted, prohibited approach

#### Lockout/Tagout Procedures
- **NEC Article 750**: LOTO for traffic control systems
- **Energy Control Program**: Written procedures
- **Verification**: Zero energy state verification
- **Training**: Qualified worker training

### 3. Fall Protection Planning

#### Fall Hazard Assessment
- **Pole Installation**: Fall hazards >6 feet
- **Aerial Lift Operations**: Platform work requirements
- **Ladder Work**: Climbing procedures
- **Roof Work**: Cabinet roof access

#### Fall Protection Systems
- **Personal Fall Arrest**: Harness and lanyard systems
- **Positioning Systems**: Work positioning lanyards
- **Guardrails**: Temporary guardrails
- **Safety Nets**: Where required

### 4. Emergency Response Procedures

#### Emergency Action Plan
- **Medical Emergencies**: First aid and CPR
- **Fire Response**: Fire extinguishers and procedures
- **Electrical Emergencies**: Shock and arc flash response
- **Vehicle Accidents**: Vehicle strike response

#### Rescue Procedures
- **Confined Space Rescue**: For pull boxes
- **Fall Rescue**: Aerial rescue procedures
- **Electrical Rescue**: Live voltage rescue
- **Emergency Contacts**: Emergency contact list

## Deliverables

### Primary Deliverables

1. **Traffic Control Plan Package**
   - Site-specific TCP for each location
   - Pedestrian accommodation plan
   - Flagging procedures
   - Agency approvals

2. **Electrical Safety Plan**
   - Arc flash hazard analysis
   - PPE requirements
   - LOTO procedures
   - Qualified worker requirements

3. **Fall Protection Plan**
   - Fall hazard assessment
   - Protection systems design
   - Rescue procedures
   - Training requirements

4. **Emergency Response Plan**
   - Emergency procedures
   - Emergency contact list
   - First aid requirements
   - Rescue procedures

5. **Safety Documentation Package**
   - Safety inspection checklist
   - Incident reporting procedures
   - Safety meeting schedule
   - Training records

## Technical Requirements

### Design Standards
- **OSHA 29 CFR 1926**: Construction safety standards
- **OSHA 29 CFR 1910**: General industry standards
- **NFPA 70E**: Electrical safety in the workplace
- **MUTCD Part 6**: Temporary traffic control

## Success Criteria

- **Safety Plan Completion**: All safety plans complete
- **Regulatory Compliance**: OSHA and MUTCD compliance
- **Worker Training**: All workers trained
- **Agency Approval**: Traffic control plans approved
