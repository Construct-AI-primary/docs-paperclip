# TRAFFIC-006: Environmental Impact & Permitting

## Issue Summary

Conduct environmental impact assessment and obtain necessary permits for traffic signal installations. This phase ensures environmental compliance through proper assessment, stormwater management, and coordination with regulatory agencies.

## Objectives

- Assess environmental impacts per NEPA
- Develop stormwater pollution prevention plans
- Obtain all required environmental permits
- Ensure erosion and sediment control
- Coordinate with regulatory agencies

## Scope of Work

### 1. Environmental Impact Assessment

#### NEPA Compliance
- **Categorical Exclusion**: Most traffic signals qualify
- **Environmental Assessment**: If required
- **Agency Coordination**: Lead agency consultation
- **Impact Mitigation**: Mitigation measures

### 2. Stormwater Management

#### SWPPP Development
- **NPDES Requirements**: Construction stormwater permit
- **BMP Selection**: Erosion and sediment controls
- **Inspection Plan**: Regular inspection schedule
- **Maintenance**: BMP maintenance requirements

### 3. Permitting

#### Federal Permits
- **Section 404**: Army Corps wetlands permit
- **Section 401**: State water quality certification

#### State/Local Permits
- **Stormwater Permit**: State construction stormwater
- **Grading Permit**: Local excavation permit
- **Right-of-Way**: Public property access

## Deliverables

1. **Environmental Assessment**
   - NEPA compliance documentation
   - Impact analysis and mitigation

2. **SWPPP Package**
   - Stormwater pollution prevention plan
   - BMP details and inspection plan

3. **Permit Package**
   - All permit applications
   - Agency approvals and correspondences

## Success Criteria

- **Environmental Compliance**: Full NEPA compliance
- **Permits Obtained**: All permits secured
- **Agency Approval**: Regulatory approval obtained
