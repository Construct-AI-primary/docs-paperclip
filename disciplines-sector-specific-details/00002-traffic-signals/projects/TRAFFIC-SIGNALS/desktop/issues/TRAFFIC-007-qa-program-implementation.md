# TRAFFIC-007: QA Program Implementation

## Issue Summary

Implement quality assurance program for traffic signal installations. This phase establishes QA procedures, inspection protocols, and quality management systems to ensure all work meets specifications.

## Objectives

- Establish QA procedures and responsibilities
- Develop inspection and test plans (ITPs)
- Implement quality management system
- Train quality personnel
- Ensure documentation control

## Scope of Work

### 1. QA Program Elements

#### Quality Management System
- **ISO 9001 Framework**: Quality management principles
- **Documentation**: Document control procedures
- **Records**: Quality records management
- **Audits**: Internal audit program

### 2. Inspection and Test Plans

#### ITP Development
- **Hold Points**: Critical inspection points
- **Witness Points**: Inspection witness points
- **Acceptance Criteria**: Clear acceptance criteria
- **Testing Protocols**: Required tests and methods

### 3. Material Verification

#### Material Submittals
- **Submittal Schedule**: Required material submittals
- **Technical Data**: Product data sheets
- **Certifications**: Manufacturer certifications
- **Testing**: Pre-qualification testing

## Deliverables

1. **QA Program Documentation**
   - QA procedures manual
   - Roles and responsibilities
   - Training requirements

2. **ITP Package**
   - Inspection and test plans
   - Checklists and forms
   - Acceptance criteria

3. **Material Verification Package**
   - Submittal requirements
   - Testing protocols
   - Verification records

## Success Criteria

- **QA Program**: Complete QA system established
- **ITP Coverage**: All activities covered by ITPs
- **Personnel Trained**: QA personnel trained and qualified
