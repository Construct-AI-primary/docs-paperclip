# TRAFFIC-011: Operations & Maintenance Planning

## Issue Summary

Develop comprehensive operations and maintenance plans for traffic signal installations. This phase ensures long-term successful operation through proper planning, documentation, and training.

## Objectives

- Develop operations procedures
- Create preventive maintenance schedules
- Plan spare parts inventory
- Train operations personnel
- Establish performance monitoring

## Scope of Work

### 1. Operations Procedures

#### Standard Operating Procedures
- **Startup Procedures**: Daily startup sequence
- **Shutdown Procedures**: Controlled shutdown
- **Emergency Procedures**: Emergency response
- **Monitoring Procedures**: Performance monitoring

### 2. Preventive Maintenance

#### Maintenance Schedule
- **Weekly**: Routine inspections
- **Monthly**: Component inspection
- **Quarterly**: System testing
- **Annual**: Comprehensive inspection

#### Maintenance Tasks
- **Signal Heads**: Visual inspection and cleaning
- **Controller**: Programming verification
- **Detection**: Sensitivity adjustment
- **Electrical**: Connection inspection

### 3. Spare Parts Planning

#### Spare Parts Inventory
- **Critical Parts**: Controller, conflict monitor
- **Common Parts**: Load switches, relays
- **Consumables**: Lamps, fuses
- **Specialty Parts**: Detection equipment

### 4. Performance Monitoring

#### Monitoring Program
- **Uptime Tracking**: System availability
- **Fault Tracking**: Fault frequency and types
- **Response Time**: Maintenance response
- **Performance Reports**: Monthly reporting

## Deliverables

1. **O&M Manual Package**
   - Operations procedures
   - Maintenance procedures
   - Emergency procedures
   - Troubleshooting guide

2. **Maintenance Schedule Package**
   - Preventive maintenance schedule
   - Inspection checklists
   - Test procedures

3. **Spare Parts Package**
   - Spare parts list
   - Critical parts inventory
   - Ordering procedures

4. **Training Package**
   - Training materials
   - Video tutorials
   - Hands-on training

5. **Performance Monitoring Package**
   - Monitoring procedures
   - Reporting templates
   - Performance metrics

## Success Criteria

- **O&M Manual Complete**: Comprehensive operations guide
- **Maintenance Schedule**: Preventive schedule established
- **Spare Parts**: Adequate inventory planned
- **Personnel Trained**: Operations staff trained
