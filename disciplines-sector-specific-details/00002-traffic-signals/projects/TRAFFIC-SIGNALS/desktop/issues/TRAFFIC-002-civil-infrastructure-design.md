# TRAFFIC-002: Civil Infrastructure Design

## Issue Summary

Design comprehensive civil infrastructure for traffic signal installations including signal pole foundations, conduit and duct bank systems, trenching and backfill, pavement restoration, and ADA-compliant pedestrian facilities. This phase translates site assessment findings into detailed construction documents.

## Objectives

- Design signal pole foundations for all pole types
- Design conduit and duct bank systems
- Develop trenching and excavation specifications
- Design pavement restoration details
- Ensure ADA-compliant pedestrian facilities
- Prepare construction documents and specifications

## Scope of Work

### 1. Signal Pole Foundation Design

#### Foundation Types
- **Cast-in-Place Concrete**
  - Standard foundations for signal poles
  - 24" to 48" diameter depending on pole type
  - Reinforcement design per AASHTO requirements

- **Drilled Shaft Foundations**
  - For large mast arm poles
  - 24" to 60" diameter shafts
  - Socket design for rock conditions

- **Anchor Bolt Foundations**
  - Pre-formed anchor bolt assemblies
  - Grout specifications
  - Template requirements

#### Foundation Analysis
- **Load Calculations**: Dead load, wind load, seismic load
- **Soil Capacity**: Allowable bearing pressure
- **Overturning Analysis**: Factor of safety calculations
- **Sliding Resistance**: Lateral load resistance

### 2. Conduit and Duct Bank Design

#### Conduit System Design
- **Material Selection**: PVC-coated rigid steel or Schedule 80 PVC
- **Size Determination**: Based on conductor count and fill
- **Bend Radius**: Minimum radius specifications
- **Fitting Requirements**: Weatherproof and concrete-tight

#### Duct Bank Design
- **Configuration**: Number and arrangement of conduits
- **Spacing**: Minimum separation between conduits
- **Warning Tape**: Buried warning tape placement
- **Pull Box Sizing**: Based on conduit count and wire size

#### Trench Design
- **Width**: Minimum width calculations
- **Depth**: Minimum cover requirements
- **Bedding**: Bedding material specifications
- **Backfill**: Select material requirements

### 3. Pavement Restoration Design

#### Temporary Restoration
- **Cold Mix Asphalt**: Temporary patch specifications
- **Traffic Control**: Duration and placement
- **Maintenance**: Temporary patch maintenance requirements

#### Permanent Restoration
- **Hot Mix Asphalt**: Match existing pavement
- **Full-Depth Concrete**: For concrete pavements
- **Match Requirements**: Match existing type and thickness
- **Joint Details**: Joint spacing and sealing

### 4. ADA-Compliant Pedestrian Facilities

#### Pedestrian Push Button Installation
- **Height**: 42" to 48" above grade
- **Reach**: Maximum 10" horizontal reach
- **Clear Floor Space**: 30"x48" clear space
- **Accessible Path**: Connection to accessible route

#### Pedestrian Signal Installation
- **Height**: 7' to 10' above grade
- **Location**: Near each end of crosswalk
- **Clear Width**: Minimum 4' clear width
- **Tactile Features**: Tactile arrows for VI users

#### Crosswalk Design
- **Width**: Minimum 8' wide, 10' preferred
- **Markings**: White thermoplastic crosswalk markings
- **Detectable Warnings**: Truncated domes at curb cuts
- **Slope Requirements**: Maximum 1:12 running slope

### 5. Stormwater Management

#### Surface Drainage
- **Grading**: Positive drainage away from foundations
- **Drainage Patterns**: Maintain existing drainage
- **Inlet Protection**: Protect existing inlets

#### Erosion Control
- **Silt Fence**: Perimeter protection
- **Inlet Protection**: Protect storm drains
- **Stabilization**: Temporary seeding where applicable

### 6. Construction Documentation

#### Plans
- **Site Plan**: Overall site layout
- **Foundation Plan**: Foundation locations and details
- **Conduit Plan**: Conduit routing and sizes
- **Grading Plan**: Final grades and drainage
- **Restoration Plan**: Pavement restoration limits

#### Specifications
- **General**: Standard specification sections
- **Material Specifications**: Material requirements
- **Construction Specifications**: Installation requirements
- **Quality Requirements**: Testing and inspection

#### Cost Estimate
- **Quantity Takeoff**: Measurement of all items
- **Unit Prices**: Current unit pricing
- **Contingencies**: Appropriate contingency allowances
- **Engineer's Estimate**: Total project cost
