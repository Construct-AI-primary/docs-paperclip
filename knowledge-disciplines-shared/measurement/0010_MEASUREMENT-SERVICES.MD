# Measurement Services Project Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Measurement services procurement covers surveying equipment, metrology tools, calibration standards, inspection instruments, and professional measurement services across engineering, construction, and quality assurance disciplines.

## Common PO Types

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| Surveying Equipment | Total stations, GPS/GNSS receivers, laser scanners, levels, drones | Accuracy mm, range, data logging, IP rating, battery life | 4-12 weeks |
| Calibration Standards | Gauge blocks, reference standards, thermocouples, weights | Accuracy class, traceability cert, temperature range | 4-10 weeks |
| Dimensional Tools | Calipers, micrometers, CMM probes, laser trackers | Resolution, range, accuracy, software compatibility | 2-6 weeks |
| Inspection Equipment | Borescopes, ultrasonic flaw detectors, coating gauges, hardness testers | Measurement type, probe options, data storage, certification | 4-8 weeks |
| NDT Equipment | X-ray, magnetic particle, dye penetrant, eddy current | Sensitivity, power output, regulation compliance, reporting | 6-16 weeks |

## Common WO Types

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Land Surveying | Topographic, cadastral, engineering surveys, as-built verification | Surveyor registration, accuracy standards, datum coordination |
| Dimensional Inspection | First article inspection, in-process inspection, final QC | ISO 9001, AS9102 FAI, inspection plan, NIST traceability |
| Calibration Services | On-site calibration, lab calibration, adjustment, certification | ISO 17025 accreditation, calibration interval, uncertainty budget |
| Quantity Verification | Bulk material measurement, steel tonnage verification, volume calculation | Measurement methods, reporting format, dispute resolution |
| NDT Services | UT, RT, MT, PT, PAUT, TOFD inspection services | NDT Level II/III certification, procedure qualification, reporting |

## Key Distinctions
### Goods (PO Items)
- Surveying equipment with calibration certs and manufacturer support
- NDT equipment with certification and regulatory compliance
- Calibration standards with traceability and uncertainty documentation

### Services (WO Items)
- Surveying with registered professionals and datum coordination
- Calibration with ISO 17025 accreditation and traceability
- NDT with certified technicians, procedure qualification, and documented results

## Project-Specific Considerations
- **Construction:** Setting out, as-built surveys, deformation monitoring, volumetric measurements
- **Manufacturing:** First article inspection, in-process QC, CMM programming, PPAP
- **Oil & Gas:** Pipeline alignment, tank calibration, crack detection, corrosion mapping
- **Civil Infrastructure:** Road alignment, bridge deflection, tunnel profiling, settlement monitoring
