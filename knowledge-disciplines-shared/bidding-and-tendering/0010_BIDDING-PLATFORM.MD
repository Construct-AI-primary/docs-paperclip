# Bidding & Tendering Project Procurement Typology

## Sector Overview
Bidding and tendering procurement covers e-tendering software platforms, document management systems, bid evaluation tools, and professional tender management services. This shared discipline supports procurement across all construction and engineering project types.

## Common PO Types

| PO Category | Typical Goods | Key Specifications | Lead Times |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|------------|
| E-Tendering Software | Tender management platforms, e-submission portals | User count, concurrent tenders, document storage, audit trail | 2-6 weeks |
| Document Management | EDMS platforms, document control software, version control | Storage capacity, workflow automation, compliance cert, API access | 2-6 weeks |
| Evaluation Tools | Bid evaluation software, scoring matrices, weighted analysis | Configurable criteria, automated scoring, reporting, audit trail | 2-4 weeks |
| Contract Repository | Contract management systems, clause libraries, template management | Search capabilities, version control, expiry alerts, integration | 4-8 weeks |

## Common WO Types

| WO Category | Typical Services | Compliance Requirements |
|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Tender Management | Tender preparation, advertising, clarification, receipt, opening | PPPFA/POPIA, transparency, confidentiality, timeline compliance |
| Bid Evaluation | Technical evaluation, commercial evaluation, B-BBEE verification | Evaluation criteria, scoring methodology, conflict of interest mgmt |
| Negotiation Support | Commercial negotiation, contract award recommendation | Delegation of authority, negotiation mandate, documentation |
| Award Administration | Letter of award, contract signing, debriefing notifications | Regulatory compliance, timeframe, debriefing records |

## Key Distinctions
### Goods (PO Items)
- Software platforms with configuration, training, and support requirements
- Document management with compliance and audit trail requirements

### Services (WO Items)
- Tender management with regulatory compliance and transparency requirements
- Bid evaluation with documented criteria, scoring, and conflict of interest management
- Award administration with formal letters, debriefings, and dispute resolution

## Project-Specific Considerations
- **Public Sector:** Full compliance with procurement regulations, B-BBEE scoring, local content requirements
- **Private Sector:** Negotiated procedures, framework agreements, strategic sourcing
- **EPC/Turnkey:** Pre-qualification, technical qualification, financial capability assessment
- **Design-Build:** Quality-based selection, technical proposal evaluation, design methodology scoring
