# LOOPY-001: Mining Executive Persona Analysis for AI Native Platform Campaign

## Issue Summary

Conduct comprehensive research and analysis of mining executive personas to understand their motivations, pain points, and technology adoption patterns. This foundational analysis will inform the creation of compelling LinkedIn campaign content that resonates with mining executives and effectively communicates the value of AI native platforms for modern mining operations.

## Background

Loopy AI's first project focuses on creating high-converting LinkedIn campaign content for mining executives about AI native platforms. To create content that drives engagement and conversion, we need deep understanding of:

- Mining executive decision-making processes and priorities
- Technology adoption barriers and drivers in mining operations
- Pain points related to operational efficiency and digital transformation
- Communication preferences and professional networks on LinkedIn
- Value propositions that resonate with mining industry leaders

## Requirements

### Mining Executive Persona Framework

#### 1. Executive Profile Development
```typescript
interface MiningExecutivePersona {
  role: 'CEO' | 'COO' | 'CTO' | 'CFO' | 'Operations Director' | 'IT Director';
  companySize: 'Small' | 'Medium' | 'Large' | 'Major';
  experience: number; // years in mining
  technologyMaturity: 'Traditional' | 'Digital' | 'Advanced' | 'Leading';
  decisionDrivers: DecisionDriver[];
  painPoints: PainPoint[];
  communicationStyle: CommunicationStyle;
}
```

#### 2. Decision Driver Analysis
- **Operational Efficiency:** Cost reduction, productivity improvement, safety enhancement
- **Digital Transformation:** Technology modernization, data-driven decision making
- **Competitive Advantage:** Market positioning, innovation leadership, talent attraction
- **Risk Management:** Regulatory compliance, operational resilience, sustainability
- **Financial Performance:** ROI optimization, cost control, revenue growth

#### 3. Technology Adoption Assessment
- **Current Technology Stack:** ERP systems, SCADA, IoT sensors, data analytics
- **AI Readiness:** Understanding of AI capabilities, previous AI implementations
- **Adoption Barriers:** Legacy systems, regulatory concerns, skill gaps, cost concerns
- **Success Factors:** Proven ROI, industry validation, implementation support

#### 4. LinkedIn Engagement Patterns
- **Content Preferences:** Industry insights, case studies, executive interviews
- **Engagement Triggers:** Operational challenges, competitive threats, regulatory changes
- **Network Composition:** Industry peers, technology vendors, consultants, analysts
- **Posting Frequency:** Optimal timing and frequency for maximum reach
