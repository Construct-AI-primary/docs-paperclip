# DEVFORGE-GOAL-001: Provide AI-Native Solutions Across All Business Disciplines Worldwide

## Goal Statement

Establish DevForge AI as the global leader in AI-native business solutions, providing intelligent automation and optimization across all 50+ business disciplines and departments, enabling organizations worldwide to transform their operations through AI-first thinking and implementation.

## Background

Traditional business software is being disrupted by AI-native solutions that are built from the ground up with AI capabilities. DevForge AI will lead this transformation by developing comprehensive AI solutions for every major business discipline, from commercial and construction to healthcare and finance. Our intelligent model selection system is just the first step in this broader mission.
