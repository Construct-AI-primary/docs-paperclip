# Zama Traffic Signals CC

Zama Traffic Signals CC (branded as Zama Traffic Signals / Zama Traffic Signals High Voltage Systems) is a South African electrical-engineering contractor that specialises in traffic-signal and electrical infrastructure for municipalities, road authorities, and utilities.

## Company Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Legal Name** | Zama Traffic Signals CC |
| **Brands** | Zama Traffic Signals, Zama Traffic Signals High Voltage Systems |
| **Established** | 2005 |
| **Ownership** | 100% Black-Owned and Managed |
| **Location** | 1 Glen Anil Street, Durban North, Durban, South Africa |
| **Contact** | info@zamatrafficsignals.co.za |
| **Owner** | Zamile Bovungana |

## Core Business

Zama operates as an electrical-engineering contractor whose core business sits at the intersection of:

1. **Traffic-Signal Infrastructure** — Electrical systems for traffic-signal installations
2. **MV/LV Power Networks** — Medium and low-voltage network construction and maintenance
3. **High-Voltage Systems** — Substation installations, switchgear, and protection systems
4. **Cable-Fault Services** — Location, diagnosis, and repair of cable faults

## Target Market

- **Primary Clients:** Municipalities, SANRAL, provincial road authorities, transport-related public agencies
- **Geographic Footprint:** South Africa-wide, with particular focus on KwaZulu-Natal and Gauteng infrastructure corridors
- **Market Position:** Mid-sized B-BBEE-aligned electrical contractor attractive to public-sector tenders

## Key Differentiators

1. **100% Black-Owned** — Aligns with South African B-BBEE and preferential-procurement policies
2. **Niche Specialisation** — Focus on traffic-signal power systems, MV/LV networks, and substation-related work
3. **Mid-Scale Capability** — Capable of handling multiple concurrent electrical and traffic-signal projects
4. **Disciplined Contract Management** — On-time, on-budget delivery with compliance focus
