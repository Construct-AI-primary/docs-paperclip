# SANRAL Electrical Projects

## Overview

Zama Traffic Signals' SANRAL projects involve the routine maintenance and upgrade of electrical lighting infrastructure on national roads across South Africa.

## Project Categories

### Road Lighting Maintenance
- Routine inspection and maintenance
- Lamp replacement
- Pole maintenance
- Cable fault repairs

### Electrical Infrastructure
- MV/LV network maintenance
- Substation maintenance
- Protection system maintenance
- Cable fault location and repair

### N-Route Projects
- National route lighting maintenance
- Interchange lighting
- Rest area electrical
- Toll plaza electrical

## Client

- SANRAL (South African National Roads Agency)
