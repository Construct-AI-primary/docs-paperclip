# Mining Infrastructure Projects

## Overview

GPC Guinea's mining infrastructure projects involve road construction, site preparation, and civil works for mining companies operating in Guinea.

## Project Categories

### Mine Access Roads
- Haul road construction
- Access road maintenance
- Heavy-duty pavement
- Bridge construction

### Site Preparation
- Land clearing
- Earthworks
- Platform construction
- Drainage installation

### Mining Facilities
- Camp construction
- Warehouse buildings
- Processing facility support
- Utility infrastructure

## Client Types

- Bauxite mining companies
- Iron ore mining companies
- Gold mining companies
- Diamond mining companies
