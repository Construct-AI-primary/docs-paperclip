# Government Infrastructure Projects

## Overview

GPC Guinea's government infrastructure projects involve road construction, rehabilitation, and maintenance for Guinean national and local government agencies.

## Project Categories

### Road Construction
- National road construction
- Regional road development
- Urban road construction
- Rural access roads

### Road Rehabilitation
- Pavement rehabilitation
- Bridge repair and rehabilitation
- Drainage system rehabilitation
- Safety improvement works

### Road Maintenance
- Routine road maintenance
- Periodic maintenance
- Emergency repairs
- pothole patching

## Client Types

- Ministry of Public Works
- National roads authority
- Regional governments
- Municipal authorities
