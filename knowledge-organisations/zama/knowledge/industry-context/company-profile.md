# Zama Traffic Signals CC - Company Profile

## Executive Summary

Zama Traffic Signals CC (often branded as Zama Traffic Signals / Zama Traffic Signals High Voltage Systems) is a South African electrical-engineering contractor established in 2005. The company specialises in traffic-signal infrastructure, MV/LV network systems, and high-voltage electrical work for municipalities, road authorities, and utilities across South Africa.

## Business Sector

Zama operates at the intersection of **electrical engineering** and **traffic infrastructure**, positioning itself as a specialist contractor rather than a generic construction or traffic-control firm.

### Primary Business Activities

1. **Electrical and Traffic-Signal Engineering**
   - Design, installation, and commissioning of traffic-signal power and control infrastructure
   - MV (medium-voltage) and LV (low-voltage) network construction
   - Cable-fault location, diagnosis, and repair
   - Protection-system maintenance and switchgear installation

2. **Substation and High-Voltage Systems**
   - Substation installations and upgrades
   - Transformer and busbar installation
   - Protection-relay configuration and testing
   - High-voltage testing and commissioning

3. **Project and Contract Management**
   - Disciplined delivery on time and within budget
   - Client and environmental compliance
   - Public-sector tender execution

## Strategic Positioning

### Strengths

1. **B-BBEE Alignment** — 100% black-owned status provides advantage in public-sector tenders
2. **Niche Specialisation** — Focused depth in traffic-signal power systems vs. generalist competitors
3. **Established Track Record** — 20+ years operating in South African infrastructure market
4. **Disciplined Execution** — Emphasis on contract management and compliance

### Market Position

Zama competes not as a general construction or traffic-control firm, but as a **specialist electrical contractor** with depth in:
- Traffic-signal power systems
- MV/LV networks
- Substation-related electrical work

This positions Zama as a peer to regional electrical contractors that also service traffic-signal electrification, with particular appeal to municipalities and road authorities requiring B-BBEE-aligned contractors.
