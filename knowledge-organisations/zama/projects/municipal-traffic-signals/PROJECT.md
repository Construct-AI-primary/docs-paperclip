# Municipal Traffic Signal Projects

## Overview

Zama Traffic Signals' municipal traffic signal projects involve the design, installation, upgrade, and maintenance of traffic signal infrastructure for municipalities across South Africa.

## Project Categories

### Traffic Signal Installation
- New intersection signalisation
- Pedestrian crossing signals
- School zone signals
- Ramp signals

### Traffic Signal Upgrade
- LED signal head replacement
- Controller upgrades
- Detection system upgrades
- Communication system upgrades

### Traffic Signal Maintenance
- Routine maintenance contracts
- Fault response services
- Preventive maintenance
- Emergency repairs

## Client Types

- Metropolitan municipalities (eThekwini, Johannesburg, Cape Town)
- Local municipalities
- Provincial road authorities
- Traffic management centres
