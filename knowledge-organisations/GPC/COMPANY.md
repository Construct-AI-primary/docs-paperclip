# GPC Guinea

GPC (Guinea Project Construction) is a Guinean construction and infrastructure contractor operating in the West African construction market. The company specializes in civil engineering, building construction, and public works projects across Guinea.

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Company Name** | GPC Guinea |

| **Location** | Guinea, West Africa |
| **Focus Areas** | Civil Engineering, Building Construction, Public Works |

## Core Business

GPC operates as a construction contractor whose core business sits at the intersection of:

1. **Civil Engineering** — Infrastructure construction including roads, bridges, and drainage
2. **Building Construction** — Commercial, residential, and industrial building projects
3. **Public Works** — Government infrastructure projects and municipal works
4. **Infrastructure Development** — Large-scale infrastructure projects across Guinea

## Target Market

- **Primary Clients:** Guinean government, municipalities, international development organizations
- **Geographic Footprint:** Guinea, with potential for West African regional expansion
- **Market Position:** Local contractor with capability for medium to large infrastructure projects
