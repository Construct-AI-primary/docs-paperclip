# Solar Installations Discipline Workflows Master Catalog

## Overview

This document catalogs all solar installation workflows derived from 11 engineering disciplines. Solar installations require comprehensive coordination across electrical, civil, geotechnical, architectural, safety, environmental, QA/QC, and security disciplines to ensure safe, compliant, and efficient solar energy systems.

## Solar Installation Workflow Integration Matrix

### SOLAR-001: Site Assessment & Feasibility (Civil + Electrical + Geotechnical + Environmental)

**Primary Disciplines**: Civil (00850), Electrical (00860), Geotechnical (00855), Environmental (01000)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **CIVIL-001**: Stormwater Management Design → Solar site drainage analysis
- **ELEC-001**: Electrical Power Distribution → Grid interconnection feasibility
- **GEOTECH-001**: Foundation Design → Ground condition assessment for mounting
- **ENV-001**: Environmental Impact Assessment → Solar farm environmental scoping

**Key Deliverables**:
- Solar resource assessment report
- Site suitability analysis
- Preliminary foundation recommendations
- Environmental impact screening
- Grid interconnection feasibility study

### SOLAR-002: Structural Design & Mounting Systems (Civil + Geotechnical + Mechanical)

**Primary Disciplines**: Civil (00850), Geotechnical (00855), Mechanical (00870)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **CIVIL-003**: Earthworks Specification → Site preparation for mounting systems
- **GEOTECH-001**: Foundation Design → Pile/ground screw foundation design
- **MECH-004**: Equipment Selection & Specification → Mounting system procurement

**Key Deliverables**:
- Structural calculations for mounting systems
- Foundation design specifications
- Wind load analysis reports
- Mounting system technical specifications
- Installation methodology documents

### SOLAR-003: Electrical System Design & Grid Integration (Electrical + Design Management)

**Primary Disciplines**: Electrical (00860), Design Management (00800)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **ELEC-001**: Electrical Power Distribution → Solar array electrical design
- **ELEC-004**: Protection & Coordination → Inverter and string protection
- **DESIGN-005**: Engineering Discipline Integration Matrix → Electrical interfaces

**Key Deliverables**:
- Single-line diagrams for solar systems
- Cable sizing calculations
- Protection coordination studies
- Inverter specifications
- Grid interconnection applications

### SOLAR-004: Building Integration & Architectural Coordination (Architectural + Design Management)

**Primary Disciplines**: Architectural (00825), Design Management (00800)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **ARCH-001**: Spatial Planning & Programming → Building envelope solar integration
- **ARCH-004**: Fire Safety & Compartmentation → Solar system fire safety
- **DESIGN-001**: Design Coordination & Interface Management → Architectural interfaces

**Key Deliverables**:
- Building-integrated PV (BIPV) design specifications
- Architectural integration drawings
- Fire safety compliance documentation
- Aesthetic coordination plans
- Building permit coordination packages

### SOLAR-005: Safety & Compliance Requirements (Safety + Electrical + Design Management)

**Primary Disciplines**: Safety (02400), Electrical (00860), Design Management (00800)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **SAFETY-001**: Hazard Identification & Risk Assessment → Solar-specific hazards
- **ELEC-003**: Lighting Design → Safety lighting for solar installations
- **DESIGN-002**: Interdisciplinary Design Review → Safety integration review

**Key Deliverables**:
- Solar installation safety plans
- Arc flash hazard analysis
- Fall protection plans
- Emergency response procedures
- Safety compliance documentation

### SOLAR-006: Environmental Impact & Permitting (Environmental + Civil)

**Primary Disciplines**: Environmental (01000), Civil (00850)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **ENV-001**: Environmental Impact Assessment → Full EIA for solar projects
- **ENV-004**: Environmental Monitoring → Construction and operational monitoring
- **CIVIL-001**: Stormwater Management Design → Erosion control during construction

**Key Deliverables**:
- Environmental impact assessment reports
- Permitting documentation packages
- Erosion and sediment control plans
- Wildlife impact studies
- Environmental management plans

### SOLAR-007: QA Program Implementation (Quality Assurance + Design Management)

**Primary Disciplines**: Quality Assurance (02200), Design Management (00800)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **QA-001**: QA Program Implementation → Solar-specific QA procedures
- **QA-002**: Compliance Auditing → Regulatory compliance verification
- **DESIGN-003**: Design Programme & Deliverables → QA milestone tracking

**Key Deliverables**:
- Quality assurance program documentation
- Inspection and test plans (ITPs)
- Non-conformance management procedures
- Quality audit schedules
- Compliance verification reports

### SOLAR-008: QC Inspection & Testing (Quality Control + All Technical Disciplines)

**Primary Disciplines**: Quality Control (02250), All technical disciplines

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **QC-001**: Inspection & Testing Protocols → Solar equipment inspection
- **QC-002**: Non-Conformance Management → Defect tracking and resolution

**Key Deliverables**:
- Pre-installation inspection checklists
- Installation quality control procedures
- Commissioning test protocols
- Non-conformance reports (NCRs)
- Quality control documentation packages

### SOLAR-009: Security Systems & Access Control (Security + Design Management)

**Primary Disciplines**: Security (02500), Design Management (00800)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **SEC-001**: Threat & Risk Assessment → Solar farm security assessment
- **SEC-002**: Access Control Management → Site access procedures
- **SEC-003**: Security Systems Design → CCTV and alarm systems

**Key Deliverables**:
- Security risk assessments
- Access control plans
- Security system specifications
- Emergency response coordination
- Security monitoring procedures

### SOLAR-010: Commissioning & Testing (Electrical + Mechanical + QC)

**Primary Disciplines**: Electrical (00860), Mechanical (00870), Quality Control (02250)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **ELEC-006**: Electrical Construction Support → Electrical testing and commissioning
- **MECH-005**: Mechanical Construction Support → Mechanical system testing
- **QC-001**: Inspection & Testing Protocols → Commissioning verification

**Key Deliverables**:
- Commissioning procedures and checklists
- Performance testing protocols
- System integration testing reports
- Warranty documentation packages
- Handover certificates

### SOLAR-011: Operations & Maintenance Planning (All Disciplines + Design Management)

**Primary Disciplines**: All disciplines, Design Management (00800)

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **DESIGN-003**: Design Programme & Deliverables → O&M manual development
- **SAFETY-004**: Emergency Response Planning → Maintenance safety procedures
- **ENV-005**: Rehabilitation Planning → End-of-life planning

**Key Deliverables**:
- Operations and maintenance manuals
- Preventive maintenance schedules
- Spare parts lists and procurement plans
- Performance monitoring specifications
- End-of-life decommissioning plans

### SOLAR-012: Project Closeout & Documentation (Design Management + QA + All Disciplines)

**Primary Disciplines**: Design Management (00800), Quality Assurance (02200), All disciplines

**Integrated Workflows**:
- **DESIGN-003**: Design Programme & Deliverables → Final documentation compilation
- **QA-003**: Quality Management Systems → Final quality verification
- **All Discipline Handover Workflows**: As-built documentation

**Key Deliverables**:
- Complete as-built documentation set
- Final commissioning reports
- Warranty documentation
- Training records
- Project closeout reports

## Cross-Discipline Dependencies

### Primary Integration Points
1. **Design Management (00800)** coordinates all solar workflows
2. **Quality Control (02250)** interfaces with all construction activities
3. **Safety (02400)** integrates with all field operations
4. **Electrical (00860)** coordinates with civil, geotechnical, and mechanical
5. **Environmental (01000)** interfaces with civil and construction activities

### Workflow Integration Points
Each solar workflow documents:
- **Upstream inputs** from other disciplines
- **Downstream outputs** to dependent workflows
- **Cross-discipline interfaces** and coordination requirements
- **Key handoff points** between disciplines
