# Security Requirements for Solar PV Installations

## Overview

Solar photovoltaic installations require comprehensive security measures to protect valuable equipment, prevent theft, ensure operational integrity, and maintain public safety. This document covers physical security, cybersecurity, access control, and monitoring requirements for both ground-mounted and rooftop solar systems.

## 1. Physical Security Measures

### Perimeter Security

#### Fencing and Barriers
- **Perimeter Fencing**
  - 8-foot high chain-link fencing with barbed wire
  - Anti-climb designs and tamper-resistant features
  - Ground-mounted systems: Complete perimeter enclosure
  - Rooftop systems: Equipment room and access point security

#### Access Control
- **Gates and Entry Points**
  - Controlled access gates with electronic locking
  - Vehicle barriers and bollards
  - Visitor management systems
  - Emergency access protocols

### Equipment Protection

#### Module Security
- **Anti-Theft Measures**
  - Tamper-evident seals and security fasteners
  - Module tracking and serialization
  - Secure mounting systems resistant to removal

#### Inverter and Equipment Security
- **Equipment Enclosures**
  - Lockable cabinets and enclosures
  - Tamper alarms and monitoring
  - Secure equipment rooms with access controls
