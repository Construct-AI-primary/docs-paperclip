# Solar Installations Workflow Inventory

This document provides a comprehensive inventory of all solar installation workflows, organized by project phase and cross-discipline integration requirements.

## Workflow Overview

The solar installations project encompasses **12 integrated workflows** that coordinate across **11 engineering disciplines** to deliver complete solar energy systems from concept through operations.

### Workflow Distribution by Discipline

| Discipline | Workflows | Primary Focus |
|------------|-----------|---------------|
| **Design Management** | 1 | Cross-discipline coordination |
| **Electrical** | 1 | Power systems and grid integration |
| **Civil** | 1 | Structural and site development |
| **Geotechnical** | 1 | Foundation and ground conditions |
| **Architectural** | 1 | Building integration |
| **Safety** | 1 | Risk management and compliance |
| **Environmental** | 1 | Impact assessment and permitting |
| **Quality Assurance** | 1 | QA program management |
| **Quality Control** | 1 | Inspection and testing |
| **Security** | 1 | Access control and monitoring |
| **Mechanical** | 1 | Equipment and tracking systems |

## Phase 1: Planning & Design Workflows

### SOLAR-001: Site Assessment & Feasibility
**Primary Disciplines**: Civil, Electrical, Geotechnical, Environmental
**Platform Applicability**: Desktop, Mobile, Web
**Estimated Duration**: 4-6 weeks

**Objectives**:
- Evaluate site solar potential and constraints
- Assess grid interconnection feasibility
- Analyze geotechnical conditions for foundations
- Conduct preliminary environmental screening

**Key Deliverables**:
- Solar resource assessment report
- Site suitability analysis
- Preliminary foundation recommendations
- Environmental impact screening
- Grid interconnection feasibility study

**Dependencies**: None (project initiation)

### SOLAR-002: Structural Design & Mounting Systems
**Primary Disciplines**: Civil, Geotechnical, Mechanical
**Platform Applicability**: Desktop
**Estimated Duration**: 6-8 weeks

**Objectives**:
- Design mounting structures for solar panels
- Analyze wind and seismic loading
- Specify foundation requirements
- Optimize structural efficiency

**Key Deliverables**:
- Structural calculations and drawings
- Foundation design specifications
- Wind load analysis reports
- Material specifications and procurement packages

**Dependencies**: SOLAR-001 completion

### SOLAR-003: Electrical System Design & Grid Integration
**Primary Disciplines**: Electrical, Design Management
**Platform Applicability**: Desktop, Web
**Estimated Duration**: 8-10 weeks

**Objectives**:
- Design PV array electrical configuration
- Specify inverters and power conditioning equipment
- Coordinate protection and grounding systems
- Prepare grid interconnection applications

**Key Deliverables**:
- Single-line diagrams
- Equipment specifications
- Protection coordination studies
- Interconnection application packages

**Dependencies**: SOLAR-001, SOLAR-002

### SOLAR-004: Building Integration & Architectural Coordination
**Primary Disciplines**: Architectural, Design Management
**Platform Applicability**: Desktop
**Estimated Duration**: 4-6 weeks

**Objectives**:
- Coordinate with building design and aesthetics
- Design building-integrated PV (BIPV) systems
- Ensure compliance with building codes
- Optimize solar access and performance

**Key Deliverables**:
- Architectural integration plans
- BIPV system specifications
- Building permit coordination documents
- Aesthetic coordination studies

**Dependencies**: SOLAR-001, SOLAR-003

### SOLAR-005: Safety & Compliance Requirements
**Primary Disciplines**: Safety, Electrical, Design Management
**Platform Applicability**: Desktop, Mobile
**Estimated Duration**: 3-4 weeks

**Objectives**:
- Identify solar-specific safety hazards
- Develop safety management plans
- Ensure electrical safety compliance
- Prepare emergency response procedures

**Key Deliverables**:
- Safety management plans
- Hazard identification and risk assessments
- Electrical safety compliance documentation
- Emergency response procedures

**Dependencies**: SOLAR-002, SOLAR-003

### SOLAR-006: Environmental Impact & Permitting
**Primary Disciplines**: Environmental, Civil
**Platform Applicability**: Desktop, Web
**Estimated Duration**: 6-8 weeks

**Objectives**:
- Conduct comprehensive environmental impact assessment
- Prepare permitting documentation
- Develop environmental management plans
- Coordinate with regulatory authorities

**Key Deliverables**:
- Environmental impact assessment reports
- Permitting application packages
- Environmental management plans
- Mitigation and monitoring plans

**Dependencies**: SOLAR-001

## Cross-Discipline Integration Requirements

### Data Exchange Interfaces
- **Geotechnical → Civil**: Foundation design parameters
- **Civil → Electrical**: Structural loading and mounting requirements
- **Electrical → Design**: System specifications and interface requirements
- **Design → All**: Coordination matrices and interface registers
- **Safety → All**: Safety requirements and hazard communications
- **Environmental → All**: Environmental constraints and monitoring requirements

### Quality Gates
- **Design Review**: After Phase 1 completion
- **Pre-Construction**: After SOLAR-006 and SOLAR-007
- **Commissioning**: After SOLAR-010
- **Handover**: After SOLAR-012

### Platform Integration Points
- **Desktop → Mobile**: Design data transfer for field use
- **Mobile → Desktop**: Field data collection and updates
- **Desktop → Web**: Design and compliance data for monitoring
- **Mobile → Web**: Field data synchronization
- **Web → Desktop**: Performance data for analysis

## Resource Requirements

### Personnel Requirements
- **Project Manager**: 1 (full-time throughout)
- **Lead Engineers**: 1 per discipline (part-time)
- **QA/QC Specialists**: 2 (during construction)
- **Commissioning Engineers**: 2 (during commissioning)
- **Documentation Specialists**: 1 (ongoing)

### Technology Requirements
- **CAD Software**: AutoCAD, Revit, or equivalent
- **Electrical Design Software**: ETAP, SKM, or equivalent
- **Structural Analysis Software**: STAAD, SAP2000, or equivalent
- **PV System Design Software**: PVsyst, Helioscope, or equivalent
- **Mobile Applications**: iOS/Android compatible
- **Web Platforms**: Responsive design compatible
