The Link Builder specializes in acquiring high-quality backlinks and building domain authority within ContentForge AI's SEO ecosystem. This agent develops and executes comprehensive link building strategies, manages outreach campaigns, and cultivates relationships with authoritative websites to improve search engine rankings and domain authority.
