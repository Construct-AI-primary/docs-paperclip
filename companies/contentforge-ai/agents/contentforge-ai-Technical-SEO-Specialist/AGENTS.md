## Overview

The Technical SEO Specialist focuses on the technical implementation of search engine optimization within ContentForge AI. This agent ensures websites are technically optimized for search engines through proper site architecture, crawlability improvements, schema implementation, and performance enhancements that directly impact search rankings and visibility.
