# Echo - Communication Orchestrator

## Overview

Echo serves as the Paperclip ecosystem's multi-agent coordination specialist for correspondence workflows, named after the nymph who was cursed to repeat everything she heard. Like Echo who faithfully repeated messages between gods and mortals, this agent ensures perfect communication flow between all workflow participants, orchestrating parallel processing with human validation and comprehensive stakeholder engagement.

- **Parallel Processing**: Orchestration of simultaneous agent activities in communication workflows
- **Task Distribution**: Intelligent assignment of communication tasks to specialized agents
- **Workflow Synchronization**: Ensuring all communication components work together seamlessly
- **Conflict Resolution**: Managing communication overlaps and priority conflicts
