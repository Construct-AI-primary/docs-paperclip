# SaaSForge AI

## Company Overview

**SaaSForge AI** is a specialized autonomous AI company focused on customer onboarding automation, multi-tenant SaaS operations, and customer lifecycle management within the Paperclip ecosystem. The company provides comprehensive solutions for preparing applications for customer use, managing tenant isolation, and automating the entire customer onboarding journey.

## Mission

To revolutionize customer onboarding and SaaS operations through intelligent automation, ensuring seamless, secure, and scalable customer experiences from initial signup to full platform utilization.

## Core Capabilities

### Customer Onboarding Automation
- **Automated Tenant Provisioning**: Instant creation of isolated customer environments
- **API Clearing & Configuration**: Automated API setup and configuration for new customers
- **Database Initialization**: Secure database setup with proper access controls
- **User Account Creation**: Automated user account provisioning and role assignment

### Multi-Tenant Infrastructure Management
- **Tenant Isolation**: Complete data and resource isolation between customers
- **Scalable Architecture**: Auto-scaling infrastructure based on customer usage
- **Resource Optimization**: Efficient resource allocation and cost management
- **Performance Monitoring**: Real-time monitoring of tenant performance and health

### Customer Lifecycle Management
- **Onboarding Workflows**: Guided customer onboarding with progress tracking
- **Subscription Management**: Automated billing, plan changes, and renewals
- **Usage Analytics**: Customer usage tracking and optimization recommendations
- **Support Integration**: Automated support ticket creation and resolution

## Technology Stack

### Infrastructure & Cloud
- **Primary Cloud**: AWS (ECS, Lambda, RDS, S3, CloudFormation)
- **Multi-Cloud Support**: Azure, Google Cloud for customer preferences
- **Container Orchestration**: Kubernetes for scalable deployments
- **Infrastructure as Code**: Terraform, CloudFormation for automated provisioning

### Databases & Storage
- **Primary Database**: PostgreSQL with multi-tenant schema isolation
- **NoSQL Solutions**: MongoDB for flexible customer data storage
- **Caching Layer**: Redis for session management and performance optimization
- **Object Storage**: S3-compatible storage for customer assets

### Development & Automation
- **Backend**: Node.js, Python (FastAPI) for API services
- **Frontend**: React for admin dashboards and customer portals
- **Automation**: Custom scripts and workflows for onboarding automation
- **Monitoring**: DataDog, New Relic for comprehensive observability
