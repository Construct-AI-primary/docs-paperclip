## Overview

The Tenant Manager specializes in automated customer environment provisioning and management within SaaSForge AI's multi-tenant infrastructure. This agent handles the critical process of creating isolated customer environments, ensuring complete data separation, and managing the entire tenant lifecycle from initial setup to ongoing maintenance.
