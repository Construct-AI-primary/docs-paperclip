# MeasureForge AI

## Company Overview

**MeasureForge AI** is a specialized AI company focused on transforming construction and engineering measurement through intelligent CAD integration, visual audit trails, and automated standards compliance. The company provides end-to-end measurement intelligence from initial design capture through procurement execution.

## Mission

*"Transforming construction measurement through AI-powered intelligence, visual audit trails, and automated standards compliance"*

## Core Capabilities

### 1. CAD Measurement Intelligence
- **Conversational AI Guidance**: Natural language interaction across CAD systems
- **AI-Powered Element Detection**: Automated identification and classification of building elements
- **Visual Audit Trails**: Non-destructive overlays showing measurement decisions and standards compliance
- **Real-time Accuracy Optimization**: Continuous improvement of measurement precision

### 2. Standards Compliance Automation
- **Multi-Jurisdictional Support**: ASAQS, SANS 1200 MJ, international standards
- **Automated Validation**: Real-time compliance checking and error prevention
- **Dynamic Standards Updates**: Automatic adaptation to regulatory changes
- **Audit Trail Generation**: Complete documentation of compliance decisions

### 3. Procurement Pipeline Integration
- **Materials Quantity Extraction**: Integration with Candy and other materials tools
- **Procurement Order Generation**: Direct linkage to 01900 procurement workflows
- **Contract Tender Compilation**: Automated tender document preparation
- **Cost Optimization**: Multi-supplier analysis and negotiation support

## Agent Architecture

### Swarm Coordination Layer (3 agents)
- **CAD Measurement Orchestrator**: Coordinates measurement swarm operations
- **Quality Assurance Monitor**: Ensures measurement accuracy and compliance
- **Workflow Controller**: Manages cross-agent task distribution and synchronization

### Element Measurement Layer (15-20 agents)
- **Structural Elements**: Beams, columns, foundations, load-bearing walls
- **Architectural Elements**: Doors, windows, partitions, finishes
- **MEP Elements**: HVAC, electrical, plumbing, fire protection
- **Earthworks Elements**: Cut/fill calculations, road formations, site preparation

### Visual Intelligence Layer (4 agents)
- **Rendering Engine**: Generates visual overlays and audit trails
- **Standards Visualizer**: Displays compliance status and violations
- **Collaboration Hub**: Manages multi-user measurement sessions
- **Audit Trail Manager**: Maintains complete measurement history

### Standards & Compliance Layer (4 agents)
- **ASAQS Compliance Agent**: South African Quantity Surveying standards
- **SANS Standards Agent**: National standards compliance
- **International Standards Agent**: Global regulatory requirements
- **Dynamic Standards Monitor**: Tracks and implements standards updates

### CAD Integration Layer (7+ agents)
- **AutoCAD Integration Agent**: Primary DWG/DXF support
- **Revit Integration Agent**: BIM model processing
- **Civil 3D Integration Agent**: Civil engineering specialization
- **MicroStation Integration Agent**: Infrastructure design support
- **Additional CAD Agents**: SketchUp, ArchiCAD, SolidWorks

### Procurement Integration Layer (6 agents)
- **Procurement Order Agent**: 01900 workflow integration
- **Contract Tender Agent**: Tender compilation and preparation
- **Candy Materials Agent**: Builder materials quantity extraction
- **Supplier Integration Agent**: Supplier data and pricing management
- **Procurement Workflow Agent**: End-to-end procurement orchestration
- **Materials Analytics Agent**: Usage optimization and waste reduction
