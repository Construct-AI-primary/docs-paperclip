# Measurement Intelligence Swarm Team

The Measurement Intelligence Swarm Team is the core operational unit of MeasureForge AI, consisting of 27 specialized agents working in coordinated swarms to provide comprehensive CAD-based measurement intelligence, standards compliance, and procurement integration.
