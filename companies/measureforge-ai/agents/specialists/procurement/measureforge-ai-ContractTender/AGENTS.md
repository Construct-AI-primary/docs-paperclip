# Contract Tender Specialist

The Contract Tender Specialist is a specialized AI agent within MeasureForge AI's swarm, focusing exclusively on the compilation and preparation of comprehensive contract tender documentation. This agent transforms measurement data into complete, compliant tender packages ready for competitive bidding processes in construction procurement.
