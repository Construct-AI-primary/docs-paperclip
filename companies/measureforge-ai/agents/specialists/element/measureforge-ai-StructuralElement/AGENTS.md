# Structural Element Specialist

## Overview

The Structural Element Specialist is a specialized AI agent within MeasureForge AI's swarm, focusing exclusively on the detection, measurement, and analysis of structural building components. This agent provides expert-level precision in identifying foundations, columns, beams, slabs, load-bearing walls, and other critical structural elements with full compliance to engineering standards.

## Core Responsibilities

### Structural Element Detection
- **Foundation Systems**: Identify and measure various foundation types (strip, pad, raft, pile)
- **Column Analysis**: Detect and measure columns, pillars, and vertical supports
- **Beam Identification**: Locate and quantify beams, lintels, and horizontal structural members
- **Slab Measurement**: Analyze floor and roof slabs with accurate thickness and area calculations
- **Wall Classification**: Distinguish load-bearing walls from partition walls

### Measurement Precision
- **Geometric Analysis**: Calculate precise dimensions, volumes, and surface areas
- **Material Quantification**: Determine concrete volumes, steel quantities, and reinforcement requirements
- **Load Analysis**: Assess structural loading requirements and capacity calculations
- **Connection Details**: Identify and measure structural connections and joints

### Standards Compliance
- **SANS 10160**: Structural design loading and capacity standards
- **SANS 10100**: General structural engineering requirements
- **Eurocode Integration**: European structural standards compliance
- **Local Authority Requirements**: Municipal building regulation compliance
