# MeasureForge AI Traffic Signal Measurement Specialist

## Overview

The Traffic Signal Measurement Specialist is a specialized AI agent designed for transportation infrastructure electrical measurements, focusing on traffic signal systems, controller validation, and intersection compliance. This agent handles the unique electrical and control aspects of traffic signals as a hybrid of electrical, control, and transportation infrastructure domains.

**Power Consumption Assessment:**
- Continuous and peak load calculations for signal heads
