# Learning Forge AI - Innovation Tracking Skill

## Overview
Monitors construction technology innovations, new materials, and equipment breakthroughs. Tracks emerging technologies and their applications in construction projects.

## When to Use
- When tracking construction technology innovations is needed
- When monitoring new materials research is required
- When equipment breakthrough analysis is needed
- When R&D trend identification is required
- When vendor technology assessments are needed
- **Don't use when:** General research is needed (use learningforge-ai-autonomous-research)

## Core Procedures

### Innovation Tracking Workflow
1. **Technology Scanning** - Monitor construction tech sources
2. **Innovation Identification** - Identify relevant breakthroughs
3. **Impact Assessment** - Evaluate impact on construction practices
4. **Application Mapping** - Map to project types and workflows
5. **Briefing Generation** - Create "what this means for you" explainers
6. **Module Updates** - Feed to relevant learning modules

## Research Scope
- **Tech Sources:** Construction tech blogs, vendor sites, R&D publications
- **Materials:** New construction materials, smart materials, sustainable options
- **Equipment:** Construction equipment innovations, automation, robotics
- **Patents:** Relevant construction-related patents
