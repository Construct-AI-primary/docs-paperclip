# Learning Forge AI - Legal Research Skill

## Overview
Scans and monitors construction law changes, court rulings, and regulatory updates. Tracks legal precedents and ensures compliance with current regulations.

## When to Use
- When monitoring construction law changes is needed
- When court ruling analysis is required
- When regulatory compliance assessment is needed
- When legal precedent research is needed
- When contract law updates are required
- **Don't use when:** General research is needed (use learningforge-ai-autonomous-research), or safety regulations are needed (use learningforge-ai-safety-research)

## Core Procedures

### Legal Research Workflow
1. **Source Monitoring** - Track legal databases and court feeds
2. **Precedent Identification** - Identify relevant legal precedents
3. **Impact Assessment** - Evaluate impact on construction practices
4. **Compliance Mapping** - Map changes to compliance requirements
5. **Update Documentation** - Document findings for knowledge base
6. **Alert Generation** - Generate compliance alerts for stakeholders

### Regulatory Update Process
1. **Regulatory Scanning** - Monitor regulatory body updates
2. **Change Analysis** - Analyze impact of regulatory changes
3. **Compliance Updates** - Update compliance documentation
4. **Training Integration** - Feed updates to learning modules

## Research Scope
- **SA Construction Law:** CIDB, NHBRC, building regulations
- **Court Rulings:** Construction disputes, liability cases, contract interpretations
- **Regulatory Bodies:** National building regulations, local authority requirements
- **Industry Publications:** Legal journals, construction law reviews

### Cross-Company Legal Integration
- **knowledgeforge-ai:** Access legal knowledge base
- **learningforge-ai-autonomous-research:** Coordinate with general research
- **learningforge-ai-compliance-guard:** Validate compliance recommendations
- **devforge-ai:** Coordinate technical standards with legal requirements
