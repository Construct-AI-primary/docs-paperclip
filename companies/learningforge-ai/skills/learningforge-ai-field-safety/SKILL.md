# Learning Forge AI - Field Safety Skill

## Overview
Conducts field safety inspections and creates site safety training. Develops content for construction site hazard awareness.

## When to Use
- When field safety inspection training is needed
- When site safety content is required
- When construction hazard awareness is needed
- When field safety documentation is required

## Core Procedures

### Field Safety Workflow
1. **Site Analysis** - Analyze site safety requirements
2. **Hazard Identification** - Identify field hazards
3. **Training Content** - Create site safety content
4. **Documentation** - Develop safety documentation
5. **Update Integration** - Update with site changes
