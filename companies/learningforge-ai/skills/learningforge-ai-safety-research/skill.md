# Learning Forge AI - Safety Research Skill

## Overview
Tracks safety standards bodies, regulator notices, and accident-investigation reports. Monitors changes in safety regulations and best practices for construction projects.

## When to Use
- When tracking safety regulatory changes is needed
- When accident investigation analysis is required
- When safety standards research is needed
- When HSE best practices updates are needed
- When safety certification requirements change
- **Don't use when:** General research is needed (use learningforge-ai-autonomous-research), or legal research is needed (use learningforge-ai-legal-research)

## Core Procedures

### Safety Research Workflow
1. **Standards Monitoring** - Track SANS, ISO, and regulatory standards
2. **Regulator Notice Tracking** - Monitor safety regulator publications
3. **Accident Analysis** - Analyze accident investigation reports
4. **Best Practice Identification** - Identify emerging safety practices
5. **Training Updates** - Feed findings to safety training modules
6. **Alert Generation** - Generate safety alerts for projects

### Safety Update Process
1. **Source Review** - Scan safety publications and databases
2. **Impact Assessment** - Evaluate relevance to construction projects
3. **Compliance Mapping** - Map to HSE requirements
4. **Module Updates** - Update safety training content
5. **Certification Tracking** - Monitor certification requirements

## Research Scope
- **Standards Bodies:** SANS, ISO, OSHA, NOSA
- **Regulators:** Department of Employment and Labour, Compensation Fund
- **Accident Databases:** SACRMP, industry accident reports
- **Best Practices:** Construction industry safety innovations

### Cross-Company Safety Integration
- **learningforge-ai-field-safety-inspector:** Coordinate field findings
- **learningforge-ai-hazard-pattern-analyst:** Integrate hazard patterns
- **learningforge-ai-safety-workflow-agent:** Update safety workflows
- **qualityforge-ai:** Coordinate with quality assurance
