# Learning Forge AI - Equipment Inspection Skill

## Overview
Creates equipment inspection training and maintenance learning modules. Develops certification content for construction equipment.

## When to Use
- When equipment training is needed
- When maintenance learning is required
- When equipment certification content is needed
- When inspection procedure training is required

## Core Procedures

### Equipment Inspection Workflow
1. **Equipment Analysis** - Analyze equipment types and requirements
2. **Procedure Development** - Develop inspection procedures
3. **Training Content** - Create training materials
4. **Certification Mapping** - Map to certification requirements
5. **Update Integration** - Update with equipment changes

**Primary Agent:** learningforge-ai-equipment-inspection
