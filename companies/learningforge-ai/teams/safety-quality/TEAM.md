# Safety & Quality Team

Team focused on reducing safety and defect-related incidents, tracking inspections, and ensuring QC processes are learned.

## Team Lead

- `learningforge-ai-safety-quality-division-lead`

## Team Members

| Agent | Role | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| `learningforge-ai-field-safety-inspector` | Hazard Detection | Flag safety hazards from photos/videos/sensors |
| `learningforge-ai-hazard-pattern-analyst` | Pattern Recognition | Aggregate recurring safety issues across projects |
| `learningforge-ai-safety-training-compliance` | Certification Tracking | Track certifications and schedule refreshers |
| `learningforge-ai-equipment-inspection` | Equipment Monitoring | Monitor inspection logs and maintenance records |
| `learningforge-ai-material-defect-tracker` | Defect Ledger | Maintain project-level defect ledger |
| `learningforge-ai-quality-learning` | Defect-Based Learning | Map defects to learning modules |

## Team Goals

1. Flag safety hazards from photos/videos/sensors and create structured records
2. Aggregate recurring safety issues across projects weekly
3. Track certifications and schedule refresher tasks when thresholds hit
4. Monitor inspection logs and maintenance records for plant equipment
5. Maintain project-level defect ledger for materials
6. Map defects to learning modules and generate what-went-wrong scenarios

## Integration with Other Teams

- **Learning & Curriculum**: Provide defect and safety data for learning content
- **Knowledge-Flow & Docs**: Document findings in VFS
- **Research & Compliance**: Receive safety regulation research updates
