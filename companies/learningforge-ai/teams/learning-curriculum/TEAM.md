# Learning & Curriculum Team

Team focused on personalizing learning paths, turning gaps into micro-lessons, and tracking mastery.

## Team Lead

- `learningforge-ai-learning-division-lead`

## Team Members

| Agent | Role | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| `learningforge-ai-user-behavior-analyst` | Learning Gap Detection | Analyze user interactions and identify learning gaps |
| `learningforge-ai-interaction-pattern-coach` | Personalized Nudges | Create personalized learning nudges and micro-tasks |
| `learningforge-ai-feedback-synthesis` | Feedback Aggregation | Aggregate feedback and surface improvement suggestions |
| `learningforge-ai-curriculum-planner` | Learning Path Sequencing | Sequence learning paths per user role and project |
| `learningforge-ai-micro-lesson-generator` | Content Creation | Generate scenario-based exercises from detected gaps |
| `learningforge-ai-assessment-mastery` | Evaluation Design | Design adaptive quizzes and mastery tracking |

## Team Goals

1. Identify and summarize learning-gap patterns daily
2. Turn patterns into personalized in-app nudges and micro-tasks
3. Aggregate feedback and surface 3-5 high-impact suggestions weekly
4. Dynamically sequence learning paths per user role and project
5. Convert detected gaps into short scenario-based exercises
6. Design adaptive quizzes and flag over-confident users for refreshers

## Integration with Other Teams

- **Safety & Quality**: Receive defect and inspection data for learning content
- **Knowledge-Flow & Docs**: Use document analysis for lesson generation
- **Research & Compliance**: Incorporate AutoResearch findings into curriculum
