## Team Goals

1. Run self-improving scheduled research tasks on construction domain topics
2. Scan for new legal precedents and regulatory updates weekly
3. Monitor safety standards bodies and regulator notices
4. Track construction-tech blogs, vendors, and R&D releases
5. Use AutoResearch to enhance agent skills.md files
6. Identify missing capabilities in agent skill definitions
7. Schedule recurring research cycles and dispatch to Hermes agents

## AutoResearch Integration

This team integrates **Karpathy's AutoResearch** (https://github.com/karpathy/autoresearch) for:
- Automated literature review
- Skills gap analysis
- Content enhancement
- Continuous skills improvement
