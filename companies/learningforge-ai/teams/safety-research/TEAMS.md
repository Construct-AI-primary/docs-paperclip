# Safety Research Team

## Overview

The Safety Research Team is a specialized research team within LearningForge AI focused on addressing critical knowledge gaps for the MOBILE-SAFETY-INSPECTION project. The team conducts comprehensive research on construction safety standards, mobile UX patterns, and offline synchronization technologies.

## Team Mission

To enhance LearningForge AI capabilities through targeted research that enables successful development of construction safety inspection mobile applications, ensuring compliance with industry standards and optimal user experience.

## Team Structure

### Research Coordinator (Team Lead)
- **Agent**: learningforge-ai-research-coordinator
- **Role**: Overall research project management, methodology development, and cross-agent coordination
- **Reports to**: LearningForge AI CEO

### Specialized Research Agents

#### Safety Research Agent
- **Agent**: learningforge-ai-safety-research-agent
- **Focus**: OSHA standards, HSE regulations, construction safety compliance
- **Responsibilities**: Regulatory research, compliance frameworks, safety protocol analysis

#### Mobile Patterns Agent
- **Agent**: learningforge-ai-mobile-patterns-agent
- **Focus**: Construction industry mobile UX, glove-friendly interfaces, outdoor optimization
- **Responsibilities**: Mobile UI/UX research, field usability studies, adaptive design patterns

#### Domain Knowledge Agent
- **Agent**: learningforge-ai-domain-knowledge-agent
- **Focus**: Construction industry expertise, safety inspection procedures, equipment protocols
- **Responsibilities**: Industry knowledge synthesis, protocol documentation, expertise validation

#### Learning Integration Agent
- **Agent**: learningforge-ai-learning-integration-agent
- **Focus**: User feedback analysis, continuous learning, performance optimization
- **Responsibilities**: Feedback integration, learning loop optimization, capability enhancement
