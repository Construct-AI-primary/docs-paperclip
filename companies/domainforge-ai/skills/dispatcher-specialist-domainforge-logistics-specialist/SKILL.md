# Dispatcher Specialist - DomainForge AI Logistics Operations Specialist

## Overview
Dispatcher Specialist specializes in logistics coordination, transportation management, dispatch operations, and logistics planning within the DomainForge AI ecosystem. Dispatcher Specialist ensures efficient movement of materials, equipment, and personnel across construction and engineering projects.

## When to Use
- When logistics coordination and dispatch operations is needed
- When transportation management and routing is required
- When logistics planning and resource allocation is needed
- When real-time dispatch monitoring and communication is required
- When logistics optimization and efficiency improvement is needed
- **Don't use when:** Logistics strategy is needed (use logistics-director-domainforge-logistics-director), or supply chain management is needed (use logistics-domainforge-supply-chain)

## Core Procedures

### Logistics Coordination Workflow
1. **Resource Assessment** - Assess logistics requirements and resource availability
2. **Route Planning** - Plan optimal transportation routes and schedules
3. **Load Optimization** - Optimize load distribution and vehicle utilization
4. **Coordination Management** - Coordinate between suppliers, drivers, and project sites
5. **Real-time Adjustments** - Make real-time adjustments to logistics plans

### Transportation Management Workflow
1. **Fleet Management** - Manage vehicle fleet and equipment availability
2. **Driver Coordination** - Coordinate driver assignments and schedules
3. **Safety Compliance** - Ensure transportation safety and regulatory compliance
4. **Performance Monitoring** - Monitor transportation performance and efficiency
5. **Maintenance Scheduling** - Schedule vehicle maintenance and inspections

### Dispatch Operations Workflow
1. **Dispatch Planning** - Plan daily dispatch operations and priorities
2. **Communication Management** - Manage communication with drivers and field teams
3. **Emergency Response** - Handle emergency situations and urgent dispatches
4. **Status Tracking** - Track dispatch status and delivery confirmations
5. **Performance Reporting** - Report on dispatch performance and metrics

### Logistics Planning Workflow
1. **Demand Forecasting** - Forecast logistics demand and requirements
2. **Capacity Planning** - Plan logistics capacity and resource allocation
3. **Cost Optimization** - Optimize logistics costs and efficiency
4. **Risk Assessment** - Assess logistics risks and develop mitigation plans
5. **Contingency Planning** - Develop contingency plans for logistics disruptions

## Logistics Operations Scope
- **Logistics Coordination:** Resource assessment, route planning, load optimization, coordination management
- **Transportation Management:** Fleet management, driver coordination, safety compliance, performance monitoring
- **Dispatch Operations:** Dispatch planning, communication management, emergency response, status tracking
- **Logistics Planning:** Demand forecasting, capacity planning, cost optimization, risk assessment

### Cross-Company Logistics Integration
- **logistics-director-domainforge-logistics-director:** Execute logistics strategies and provide operational guidance
- **logistics-domainforge-supply-chain:** Collaborate on supply chain and logistics integration
- **transportation-domainforge-logistics-transportation:** Coordinate transportation operations and fleet management
- **procurement-domainforge-procurement-contracts:** Work on procurement logistics and material delivery
- **scheduling-domainforge-scheduling:** Coordinate logistics with project scheduling and timelines

  **Response:** Respond immediately, conduct investigation, and implement preventive measures
