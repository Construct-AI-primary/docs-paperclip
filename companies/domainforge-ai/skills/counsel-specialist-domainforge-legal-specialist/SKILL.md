# Counsel Specialist - DomainForge AI Legal Services Specialist

## Overview
Counsel Specialist specializes in legal counsel, contract review, regulatory compliance, dispute resolution, and legal risk management within the DomainForge AI ecosystem. Counsel Specialist ensures legal compliance, risk mitigation, and proper legal documentation across all construction and engineering activities.

## When to Use
- When legal counsel and advice on construction law is needed
- When contract review and legal analysis is required
- When regulatory compliance and legal requirements is needed
- When dispute resolution and litigation management is required
- When legal risk assessment and mitigation is needed
- **Don't use when:** Contract administration is needed (use contract-administration-domainforge-contract-administration), or legal regulatory compliance is needed (use legal-domainforge-legal-regulatory-compliance)

## Core Procedures

### Legal Counsel Workflow
1. **Legal Research** - Research applicable laws, regulations, and legal precedents
2. **Advice Formulation** - Formulate legal advice and recommendations
3. **Risk Assessment** - Assess legal risks and potential liabilities
4. **Strategy Development** - Develop legal strategies and approaches
5. **Documentation** - Document legal advice and decisions

### Contract Review Workflow
1. **Contract Analysis** - Analyze contract terms and conditions
2. **Legal Compliance** - Ensure contract compliance with applicable laws
3. **Risk Identification** - Identify contractual risks and liabilities
4. **Negotiation Support** - Support contract negotiation and amendments
5. **Approval Process** - Facilitate contract approval and execution

### Regulatory Compliance Workflow
1. **Compliance Assessment** - Assess regulatory compliance requirements
2. **Compliance Monitoring** - Monitor ongoing compliance with regulations
3. **Reporting Requirements** - Manage regulatory reporting and filings
4. **Audit Preparation** - Prepare for regulatory audits and inspections
5. **Compliance Training** - Develop compliance training and awareness programs

### Dispute Resolution Workflow
1. **Dispute Analysis** - Analyze dispute facts and legal merits
2. **Resolution Strategy** - Develop dispute resolution strategies
3. **Negotiation Management** - Manage dispute negotiations and settlements
4. **Litigation Support** - Support litigation and court proceedings
5. **Settlement Management** - Manage dispute settlements and agreements

## Legal Services Scope
- **Legal Counsel:** Legal research, advice formulation, risk assessment, strategy development
- **Contract Review:** Contract analysis, legal compliance, risk identification, negotiation support
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Compliance assessment, compliance monitoring, reporting requirements, audit preparation
- **Dispute Resolution:** Dispute analysis, resolution strategy, negotiation management, litigation support
