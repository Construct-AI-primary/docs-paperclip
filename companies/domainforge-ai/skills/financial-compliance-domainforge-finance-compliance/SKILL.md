# Financial Compliance - DomainForge AI Financial Compliance Specialist

## Overview
Financial Compliance specializes in financial compliance, regulatory reporting, audit preparation, and financial risk management within the DomainForge AI ecosystem. Financial Compliance ensures adherence to financial regulations, accurate reporting, and proper financial governance across all construction and engineering activities.

## When to Use
- When financial compliance and regulatory adherence is needed
- When financial reporting and disclosure requirements is required
- When audit preparation and financial control is needed
- When financial risk assessment and mitigation is required
- When financial governance and internal controls is needed
- **Don't use when:** General finance management is needed (use finance-director-domainforge-finance-director), or cost management is needed (use finance-domainforge-finance-cost-management)

## Core Procedures

### Financial Compliance Workflow
1. **Regulatory Assessment** - Assess applicable financial regulations and requirements
2. **Compliance Monitoring** - Monitor ongoing compliance with financial regulations
3. **Policy Implementation** - Implement financial compliance policies and procedures
4. **Training Delivery** - Deliver compliance training to finance and project teams
5. **Compliance Reporting** - Report on compliance status and issues

### Regulatory Reporting Workflow
1. **Reporting Requirements** - Identify financial reporting requirements and deadlines
2. **Data Collection** - Collect financial data for regulatory reporting
3. **Report Preparation** - Prepare accurate financial reports and disclosures
4. **Review Process** - Conduct internal reviews and quality assurance
5. **Submission Management** - Manage regulatory submission and follow-up

### Audit Preparation Workflow
1. **Audit Planning** - Plan for financial audits and examinations
2. **Documentation Review** - Review financial documentation and controls
3. **Control Testing** - Test internal financial controls and procedures
4. **Issue Resolution** - Resolve audit findings and implement corrections
5. **Audit Response** - Respond to audit inquiries and provide supporting documentation

### Financial Risk Management Workflow
1. **Risk Assessment** - Assess financial risks and compliance exposures
2. **Control Design** - Design financial controls and risk mitigation measures
3. **Monitoring Implementation** - Implement risk monitoring and early warning systems
4. **Incident Response** - Respond to financial compliance incidents and breaches
5. **Continuous Improvement** - Continuously improve financial risk management practices

## Financial Compliance Scope
- **Financial Compliance:** Regulatory assessment, compliance monitoring, policy implementation, training delivery
- **Regulatory Reporting:** Reporting requirements, data collection, report preparation, review process
- **Audit Preparation:** Audit planning, documentation review, control testing, issue resolution
- **Financial Risk Management:** Risk assessment, control design, monitoring implementation, incident response
