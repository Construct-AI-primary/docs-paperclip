# Transportation - DomainForge AI Transportation Specialist

## Overview
Transportation specializes in transportation planning, fleet management, transportation coordination, and transportation optimization within the DomainForge AI ecosystem. Transportation ensures efficient, safe, and cost-effective movement of materials, equipment, and personnel across construction and engineering projects.

## When to Use
- When transportation planning and route optimization is needed
- When fleet management and vehicle coordination is required
- When transportation coordination and scheduling is needed
- When transportation optimization and cost management is required
- When transportation safety and compliance is needed
- **Don't use when:** Logistics strategy is needed (use logistics-director-domainforge-logistics-director), or dispatch operations is needed (use dispatcher-specialist-domainforge-logistics-specialist)
