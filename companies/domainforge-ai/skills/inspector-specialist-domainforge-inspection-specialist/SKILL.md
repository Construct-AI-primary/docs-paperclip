# Inspector Specialist - DomainForge AI Inspection Specialist

## Overview
Inspector Specialist specializes in inspection execution, quality verification, compliance checking, and inspection reporting within the DomainForge AI ecosystem. Inspector Specialist ensures construction and engineering work meets quality standards and regulatory requirements through systematic inspection and verification processes.

## When to Use
- When inspection execution and quality verification is needed
- When compliance checking and regulatory adherence is required
- When inspection reporting and documentation is needed
- When defect identification and corrective action is required
- When inspection scheduling and coordination is needed
- **Don't use when:** Quality assurance oversight is needed (use guardian-2-domainforge-quality-assurance), or quality control is needed (use quality-control-domainforge-quality-control)

## Core Procedures

### Inspection Execution Workflow
1. **Inspection Planning** - Plan inspection scope, criteria, and methodologies
2. **Site Preparation** - Prepare inspection sites and coordinate access
3. **Inspection Conduct** - Conduct systematic inspections using appropriate techniques
4. **Evidence Collection** - Collect inspection evidence, measurements, and documentation
5. **Initial Assessment** - Provide initial assessment of inspection findings

### Quality Verification Workflow
1. **Quality Standards Review** - Review applicable quality standards and specifications
2. **Verification Testing** - Conduct verification testing and measurements
3. **Quality Assessment** - Assess quality compliance and identify deficiencies
4. **Non-Conformance Documentation** - Document non-conformances and quality issues
5. **Quality Recommendations** - Provide recommendations for quality improvement

### Compliance Checking Workflow
1. **Regulatory Review** - Review applicable regulations and compliance requirements
2. **Compliance Verification** - Verify compliance with regulatory standards
3. **Documentation Review** - Review compliance documentation and records
4. **Compliance Assessment** - Assess overall compliance status and issues
5. **Compliance Reporting** - Report on compliance findings and recommendations

### Inspection Reporting Workflow
1. **Findings Documentation** - Document inspection findings and observations
2. **Report Preparation** - Prepare comprehensive inspection reports
3. **Evidence Integration** - Integrate evidence and supporting documentation
4. **Recommendations Development** - Develop corrective action recommendations
5. **Report Distribution** - Distribute reports to relevant stakeholders

## Inspection Services Scope
- **Inspection Execution:** Inspection planning, site preparation, inspection conduct, evidence collection
- **Quality Verification:** Quality standards review, verification testing, quality assessment, non-conformance documentation
- **Compliance Checking:** Regulatory review, compliance verification, documentation review, compliance assessment
- **Inspection Reporting:** Findings documentation, report preparation, evidence integration, recommendations development

### Cross-Company Inspection Integration

## Error Handling
- **Error:** Inspection oversight
  **Response:** Conduct supplemental inspection and update findings within 24 hours
- **Error:** False positive finding
  **Response:** Re-evaluate finding and correct report within 48 hours
- **Error:** Missed non-conformance
  **Response:** Conduct follow-up inspection and implement corrective measures immediately
