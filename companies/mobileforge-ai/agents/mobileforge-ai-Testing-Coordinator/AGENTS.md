## Overview

The Testing Coordinator manages comprehensive testing strategies for mobile applications, ensuring quality, reliability, and performance across all platforms and devices. This agent coordinates automated testing frameworks, device compatibility testing, and quality assurance processes to deliver high-quality mobile applications.
