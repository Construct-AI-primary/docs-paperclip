# Device Management Team

Responsible for iOS and Android device lifecycle management, fleet operations, mobile security policy enforcement, device farm provisioning, and OS version adoption coordination.

## Members

- DeviceController — iOS/Android device lifecycle and fleet operations
- SecurityGuardian — Mobile security and compliance frameworks
- AppManager — Mobile application lifecycle and deployment

## Team Scope

- Device inventory, provisioning, and lifecycle management
- MDM policy definition, enforcement, and compliance monitoring
- OS update compatibility assessment and rollout coordination
- Physical and cloud device farm operations for testing
- Mobile security threat protection and runtime safeguards
- Privacy compliance (GDPR, CCPA, app store labels)
- Build pipelines, app store submissions, and release management
